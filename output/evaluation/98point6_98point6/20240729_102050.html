98point6 / 98point6

The Reddit threads discuss user experiences with 98point6, an online medical service that offers affordable consultations and prescriptions. Users highlight the service's accessibility and effectiveness for basic medical needs, while also noting limitations in prescribing medications and handling more serious health issues. There is no information available regarding employee experiences or investor perspectives in the threads reviewed.
