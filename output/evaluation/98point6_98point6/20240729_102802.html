98point6 / 98point6

The Reddit threads discuss user experiences with 98point6, an online medical service that offers affordable consultations and prescriptions. Users generally appreciate the accessibility and affordability of the service, although some express concerns about limitations in prescribing certain medications. There is no significant information available regarding employee experiences or investor perspectives.
