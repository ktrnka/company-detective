About TFS Wealth Management

TFS Wealth Management is a financial services firm based in Lincroft, New Jersey. The company operates as a full-service broker-dealer through its affiliation with World Investments, Inc., a member of FINRA and SIPC. Additionally, TFS Wealth Management offers investment advisory services through World Advisory Services, an SEC Registered Investment Advisor (TFS Wealth Management, 2023).
