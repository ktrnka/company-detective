98point6 / 98point6

The Reddit threads discuss user experiences with 98point6, an online medical service that offers affordable consultations and prescriptions. Users generally appreciate the accessibility and affordability of the service, but some express concerns about limitations in prescribing medications and the effectiveness of the service for certain health issues. There is no significant information available regarding employee experiences or investor perspectives in the threads reviewed.
