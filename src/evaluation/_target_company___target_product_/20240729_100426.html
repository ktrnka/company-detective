98point6 / 98point6

The Reddit threads discuss user experiences with 98point6, an online medical service that offers affordable consultations and prescriptions. Users generally appreciate the accessibility and cost-effectiveness of the service, particularly for minor health issues and mental health support. However, there are concerns regarding the limitations in the types of prescriptions provided and the service's hesitance to prescribe medications. There is no information available regarding employee experiences or investor perspectives in the threads reviewed.
