Instacart / Instacart

The Reddit threads discuss various aspects of Instacart, focusing on user experiences, employee perspectives, and investor insights. Users express frustration over declining service quality, increased fees, and issues with shopper behavior, while employees highlight concerns about pay and treatment. Investors note slow growth and rising costs as challenges for the company.
