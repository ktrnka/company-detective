Instacart / Instacart

The Reddit threads discuss various aspects of Instacart, focusing on user experiences, employee perspectives, and investor insights. Users express frustration over declining service quality, reduced earnings for shoppers, and issues with pricing and delivery. Employees highlight concerns about pay and treatment, while investors note slow growth and challenges in maintaining customer loyalty amidst rising competition and operational issues.
