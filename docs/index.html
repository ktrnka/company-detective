About Company Detective beta

Company Detective is meant for the late stages of an interview process. It helps me prioritize which interviews to prepare for, filter out some companies with red flags, ask better questions of interviewers, and make a more informed decision. Ideally, I hope it will reduce the number of surprises after joining a company.

The data is sourced from a variety of places, including Glassdoor, news sources, Reddit, app stores, and the company's own website.

It works best for medium-sized tech companies right now, though I'm working on expanding the coverage to different company sizes and different industries.
