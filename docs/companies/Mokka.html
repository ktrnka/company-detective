About Mokka

Mokka is a company that specializes in enhancing the efficiency of talent acquisition (TA) teams through the use of artificial intelligence. The company focuses on streamlining the hiring process, allowing teams to concentrate on engaging with top candidates rather than being overwhelmed by repetitive tasks.

Company History and Services

Mokka's primary service offering is an AI-driven platform designed to assist talent teams in several key areas:

  • Reducing Time on Repetitive Tasks: The platform automates many of the routine tasks that can consume a significant amount of time for TA teams.
  • Filtering Applications: It helps in sifting through large volumes of applications to identify the most relevant candidates, thereby improving the efficiency of the hiring process.
  • Enhancing Engagement: The platform is designed to improve engagement with top talent, ensuring that high-quality candidates are not lost in the process.

Products

The main product offered by Mokka is its AI platform, which addresses several common challenges faced by talent acquisition teams:

  • Managing High Volumes of Applications: The platform helps in dealing with overwhelming numbers of irrelevant applications.
  • Processing the Candidate Funnel: It aids in managing the entire candidate funnel more effectively.
  • Engaging with High-Quality Candidates: The platform enhances the ability of TA teams to engage with top-tier candidates.

Customers

Mokka's solutions are tailored for talent acquisition teams across various industries. The company operates in a B2B model, providing its services to businesses looking to optimize their hiring processes and improve candidate engagement.

Company Culture

Mokka promotes a culture of efficiency and innovation, with a strong focus on using technology to improve traditional hiring practices.

About Mokka

Mokka is a company focused on enhancing the efficiency of talent acquisition (TA) teams through the use of artificial intelligence. The company was founded in 2023 by Max Krasnykh and is based in New York City. Mokka's primary product is an AI-driven platform designed to streamline the hiring process by automating repetitive tasks, such as screening calls and resume reviews, and improving candidate engagement. This allows talent acquisition teams to focus more on engaging with top candidates rather than being bogged down by administrative tasks (Tracxn, 2025-01-30).

Mokka operates as a business-to-business (B2B) company, serving talent acquisition teams across various industries. The platform is designed to integrate with existing recruitment tools, providing data insights and reporting to enhance hiring strategies. Despite its innovative approach, Mokka is currently an unfunded startup, ranking 336th among 2,208 active competitors in the talent acquisition space (Tracxn, 2025-01-30).

The company faces significant competition from well-established players such as Phenom, Eightfold, and SmartRecruiters, which have raised substantial funding and have a more extensive market presence (Tracxn, 2025-01-30).

Key Personnel

Max Krasnykh is the founder and CEO of Mokka. As the leader of the company, he plays a crucial role in shaping its vision and strategy, particularly as it navigates the competitive landscape of talent acquisition technology (Tracxn, 2025-01-30).
