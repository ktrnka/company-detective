About Snap Inc.

Snap Inc. is a technology company that focuses on visual communication through its flagship product, Snapchat. The company was founded in 2010 by Evan Spiegel, Bobby Murphy, and Reggie Brown and is headquartered in Santa Monica, California. Originally known as Snapchat, Inc., the company rebranded to Snap Inc. in September 2016 (Yahoo Finance, 2024).

As of December 31, 2024, Snap Inc. employs approximately 4,800 full-time employees (Yahoo Finance, 2024). The company operates in the Communication Services sector and has a market capitalization of approximately $20.14 billion (Yahoo Finance, 2024).

Snap Inc. generates revenue primarily through advertising, leveraging its large user base of over 850 million monthly active users and 443 million daily active users as of Q3 2024 (Snap Inc., 2024). The company reported a revenue of $1.373 billion for Q3 2024, marking a 15% increase from the previous year (Snap Inc., 2024).

Snapchat, the company's main product, is a multimedia messaging application that allows users to communicate through images and short videos. It features functionalities such as Stories, Snap Map, and Spotlight, which enhance user engagement and interaction. The platform is particularly popular among the 13-34 age demographic across over 25 countries (Snap Inc., 2024).

Snap Inc. also offers augmented reality (AR) solutions, including Utility Lenses, Shopping AR, and Gaming AR, which enhance real-world experiences and revolutionize e-commerce (Snap Inc., 2024). The company distributes its products through digital platforms, primarily targeting consumers (B2C).

Key Personnel

  • Evan Spiegel: Co-founder and CEO of Snap Inc. Spiegel has been instrumental in the company's growth and innovation, particularly in the areas of AR and AI. He has expressed pride in the company's progress and its commitment to creative experiences and innovation (Snap Inc., 2024).

  • Bobby Murphy: Co-founder and Chief Technology Officer. Murphy has played a key role in developing Snapchat's technology and features, contributing to the company's reputation as a leader in AR and social media.

These include expanded in-app warnings for potentially risky interactions and enhanced friending protections to limit contact with strangers (Snap Inc., 2024). The company is committed to creating a safe environment for its users while promoting responsible use of its platform.
