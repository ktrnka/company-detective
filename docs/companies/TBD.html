About TBD

TBD is a business unit under Block, Inc. (formerly Square, Inc.), established in 2021. The company is dedicated to creating a decentralized future, focusing on empowering individuals with control over their finances, data, and identity. TBD's mission is to build an inclusive financial ecosystem using open-source protocols and standards, addressing the needs of the 1.7 billion adults globally who lack access to banking systems. The company operates in the financial technology sector, offering products and services that enhance financial access and identity management.

TBD's key projects include:

  1. Web5: A decentralized platform that introduces a new identity layer for the web, enabling decentralized applications (DWAs) and protocols. It emphasizes self-sovereign identity (SSI), allowing users to manage their data independently of centralized entities.

  2. tbDEX: An open protocol designed to facilitate liquidity and trust in financial transactions, connecting legacy currencies to digital currencies without centralized intermediaries. It uses decentralized identifiers (DIDs) and verifiable credentials (VCs) to enhance security and compliance.

TBD serves a diverse range of customers, including individuals, businesses, institutions, and governments. The company fosters a collaborative and inclusive culture, encouraging contributions from a global community of developers and innovators. It actively engages with its community through events, hackathons, and forums, promoting collaboration and innovation in the decentralized identity and financial technology space.

Partnerships and Collaborations

Chipper Cash Partnership

On May 14, 2024, TBD announced a partnership with African FinTech Chipper Cash to facilitate cross-border payments across 40 African countries. This collaboration integrates Chipper Cash with TBD’s open messaging protocol, tbDEX, to enhance consumer remittances and empower small businesses. CEO Mike Brock highlighted the significance of this initiative, focusing on building out the tbDEX ecosystem in Africa (PYMNTS, 2024-05-15).

Wisp Health Partnership

TBD Health expanded its partnership with Wisp, a virtual health provider, to offer STI/STD testing kits through Wisp’s platform. This partnership aims to democratize access to sexual health care, with Wisp CEO Monica Cepak noting the importance of this initiative (MedCity News, 2024-05-15).

Research and Development Initiatives

TBD has been actively involved in research initiatives, such as the Future of Transportation Summit held in August 2024, which focused on harnessing research and technology to improve transportation systems (TBD National Center, 2024-08-13). The organization also hosted a symposium on travel demand analysis, bringing together experts from academia and industry (TBD National Center, 2024-08-13).

Conclusion

TBD is positioning itself as a key player in the fintech and health sectors through innovative products like tbDEX and strategic partnerships. The company's focus on decentralized identity and cross-border payments addresses significant market needs, particularly in Africa. As TBD continues to expand its offerings and influence, it presents a compelling opportunity for prospective candidates and investors looking to engage with a forward-thinking organization.
