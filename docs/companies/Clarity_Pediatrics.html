About Clarity Pediatrics

Clarity Pediatrics is a digital health company founded on November 1, 2021, and is based in San Francisco, California (Crunchbase, 2025). The company specializes in providing telehealth services for pediatric chronic care, with a primary focus on Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) for children and teens aged 5 to 17. Clarity Pediatrics operates in California and Texas, offering virtual care without waitlists and accepting insurance, which makes their services more accessible to families (Clarity Pediatrics Summary).

The company generates revenue through its telehealth platform, which facilitates consultations with independent medical practitioners. Clarity Pediatrics is not a medical group or healthcare provider itself but acts as a facilitator for these services (Clarity Pediatrics, Privacy Policy, April 2024). The company serves a significant market, as over 11% of American children are diagnosed with ADHD, and it is in-network with all major commercial insurers in California (Clarity Pediatrics, Privacy Policy, April 2024).

Clarity Pediatrics offers a range of services, including ADHD screenings, Behavioral Parent Training (BPT), RUBI Parent Training, Homework Organization Made Easy (HOME), Managing Big Emotions (MBE), and ADHD Medication Management (Clarity Pediatrics Summary). The company plans to expand its services to address other chronic conditions such as asthma, allergies, and obesity in the future (FinSMEs, April 2024).

Key Personnel

  • Christina LaMontagne: CEO and co-founder of Clarity Pediatrics. She has a strong background in healthcare startups, having led two companies to over $100 million in annual recurring revenue (ARR) and profitability. Her experience in startups and telehealth positions Clarity Pediatrics for potential growth and innovation in pediatric care (Clarity Pediatrics Programs Overview, October 2024).

  • Alesandro Larrazabal: Chief Medical Officer, who is triple-board certified and has extensive experience in building specialty care services (Clarity Pediatrics Programs Overview, October 2024).

News

Funding and Expansion

  • On April 22, 2024, Clarity Pediatrics raised $10 million in a funding round led by Rethink Impact, with participation from Homebrew, Maverick Ventures, and January Ventures. The funds are intended to launch new clinical service lines, further develop their care platform, and expand their geographic reach (FinSMEs, April 2024).

  • Clarity Pediatrics announced the expansion of its virtual pediatric ADHD platform and the $10 million funding on April 21, 2024 (Business Wire, 2024-04-21).

Geographic Expansion

  • On January 23, 2025, Clarity Pediatrics expanded its footprint in the U.S. by bringing its industry-leading, AAP-recommended ADHD care to Texas (Business Wire, 2025-01-23).

Conclusion

Clarity Pediatrics is a promising player in the digital health space, particularly in pediatric ADHD care. With recent funding, a strong leadership team, and a clear focus on expanding its service offerings, the company is well-positioned for growth. The emphasis on a tech-enabled model and partnerships with major insurers suggest a commitment to quality care, appealing to families looking for convenient healthcare solutions. As the company continues to grow and adapt its services, it aims to transform pediatric chronic care and improve the lives of children with ADHD.
