About hyperparam

Hyperparam is a company dedicated to improving the data science process by providing tools for the exploration and curation of large machine learning (ML) datasets. The company was founded by Kenny Daniel, who has a significant background in AI and machine learning, including co-founding Algorithmia, a company in the MLOps space that was acquired by DataRobot in 2021 (MLOps.community, 2023).

Hyperparam's primary product is a browser-based user interface (UI) that allows users to interactively explore and curate large datasets. This platform supports modern data formats like Apache Parquet and employs machine learning models to assist users in identifying high-quality data, which is crucial for building effective training sets. The company serves a diverse range of customers, from large AI companies to smaller enterprise teams, all of whom require high-quality datasets for their machine learning models.

The company operates within the MLOps ecosystem, focusing on enhancing the usability of data exploration tools, which is essential for developing robust ML models. Hyperparam's approach is particularly relevant as dataset sizes continue to grow, necessitating new tools to enable advances in machine learning (MLOps.community, 2023).

Key personnel

Kenny Daniel is the founder and CEO of Hyperparam. He holds a Ph.D. in machine learning from the University of Southern California and has over 20 years of experience in AI. Daniel has been a significant contributor to the MLOps field and emphasizes the importance of data exploration in building state-of-the-art models. He has stated that "actually looking at your data is the most high-leverage move you can make for building state-of-the-art models" (MLOps.community, 2023).
