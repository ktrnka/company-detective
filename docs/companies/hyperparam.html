About hyperparam

Hyperparam is a company dedicated to improving the data science process by providing tools for the exploration and curation of large machine learning (ML) datasets. The company emphasizes the importance of data quality in developing advanced AI models and aims to empower teams to create optimal training sets. Hyperparam offers a scalable, browser-based user interface (UI) that allows users to interactively explore and curate extensive datasets. The platform utilizes modern web APIs and innovative machine learning techniques to assist users in evaluating data quality.

Products and Services

  • Browser-Based Dataset Tool: This tool enables users to load and explore datasets with billions of rows directly in the browser, using modern data formats like Apache Parquet.
  • Model-Assisted Data Curation: Hyperparam employs machine learning models to help users identify high-quality data, facilitating the construction of superior training sets.

Customers and Market

Hyperparam serves a diverse range of customers, from large-scale AI companies to small enterprise teams, all of whom require high-quality datasets for their machine learning models. The company operates in a B2B model, providing tools that enhance the data curation process for businesses involved in AI and machine learning.

Company Culture and Vision

Hyperparam promotes a culture centered around a deep understanding and interaction with data. The company believes that effective data science necessitates familiarity with training data and aims to make this process more intuitive and efficient through its tools. Looking forward, Hyperparam envisions a future where dataset-scale inference and interactive, browser-based data exploration redefine the construction and refinement of AI models. The company is committed to integrating human expertise with AI-assisted insights to streamline data cleaning and enhance the effectiveness of advanced AI models.
