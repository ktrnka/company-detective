About Akili Interactive

Akili Interactive is a digital therapeutics company that integrates neuroscience with technology and video game entertainment to develop digital medicines. The company was founded on January 1, 2011, and is known for its innovative approach to treating cognitive impairments through engaging video game experiences rather than traditional pharmaceuticals (Crunchbase, 2025).

Akili's flagship product, EndeavorRx, is a digital therapeutic designed to improve attention function in children aged 8-12 with ADHD. The company also offers EndeavorOTC, a treatment option for adults with ADHD, expanding its reach to a broader audience. Akili's products are available through licensed healthcare providers, indicating a B2C business model targeting individuals with ADHD and their families (Akili Interactive, 2024).

The company has faced challenges in establishing a sustainable business model, leading to a strategic shift towards licensing agreements and partnerships. In 2024, Akili announced a merger with Virtual Therapeutics, aiming to create a leading digital health company focused on mental health solutions through immersive gaming experiences (Mario Aguilar, STAT News, 2024-05-29).

Akili has raised a total of $270 million in funding, with significant investments in 2021 and earlier years (Crunchbase, 2025). Despite its initial success, the company has struggled with sluggish sales, leading to layoffs and a strategic pivot (Mario Aguilar, 2024-04-30).

Key Personnel

  • Matt Franklin: CEO of Akili Interactive. He has emphasized the strategic nature of the merger with Virtual Therapeutics, highlighting the company's commitment to delivering value to its stockholders (Don Tracy, PharmExec, 2024-05-31).

News

Merger with Virtual Therapeutics

In May 2024, Akili Interactive announced a definitive merger agreement with Virtual Therapeutics. This merger is expected to provide Akili shareholders with $34 million and represents a strategic pivot to enhance its offerings in the mental health space (Don Tracy, PharmExec, 2024-05-31). The merger aims to leverage Akili's expertise in digital therapeutics and Virtual Therapeutics' gaming industry experience to address mental health needs effectively (Morningstar, Inc., 2024-05-29).

Financial Challenges and Layoffs

Akili has faced financial challenges, leading to a decision to wind down its existing commercial operations and focus on strategic partnerships. In April 2024, the company announced layoffs affecting 46% of its staff as part of its efforts to explore strategic options amid sluggish sales (Mario Aguilar, 2024-04-30).

Clinical Developments

In February 2024, Akili announced positive results from a clinical trial for a localized version of EndeavorRx, known as STD-001, in partnership with Shionogi & Co. Ltd. This trial further validates the product's efficacy and safety, paving the way for potential availability in Japan (Don Tracy, PharmExec, 2024-05-31).

Conclusion

Akili Interactive is at a pivotal moment in its evolution, transitioning from a standalone company to a wholly owned subsidiary of Virtual Therapeutics. The merger aims to enhance Akili's offerings in the mental health space and address the growing demand for innovative treatment options for ADHD and other cognitive disorders. Despite financial challenges, Akili's focus on digital therapeutics positions it well within the expanding market for digital health solutions.
