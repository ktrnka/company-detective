About Logic20/20

Logic20/20 is a business and technology consulting firm founded on January 1, 2005, by Christian O’Meara. The company is headquartered in Seattle, Washington, USA (Crunchbase, 2024). Logic20/20 specializes in providing consulting services that intersect business and technology, aiming to simplify complex projects and enhance client operations through advanced analytics, digital transformation, and strategic management (Logic20/20, 2024).

Services and Products

Logic20/20 offers a comprehensive suite of consulting services, including:

  • Advanced Analytics: Custom solutions to improve decision-making.
  • Digital Transformation: Enhancing communication, collaboration, and customer experience through technology.
  • Strategy & Operations: Business strategy, process design, and change management.
  • Data Science & Predictive Analytics: Leveraging AI and machine learning for insights.
  • Technical Project/Product Management: Ensuring successful project delivery (Logic20/20, 2024).

The company does not offer specific products but provides tailored solutions using advanced technologies like AI and machine learning to meet client needs (Logic20/20, 2024).

Customers and Market

Logic20/20 serves a diverse range of industries, including energy and utilities, technology and communications, financial services, life sciences, healthcare, government, and education. Notable clients include T-Mobile and San Diego Gas and Electric (Logic20/20, 2024). The company operates primarily in a B2B model, focusing on enhancing operational efficiency and customer engagement for its clients (Built In Seattle, 2024).

Financial Performance

While specific revenue figures are not publicly disclosed, Logic20/20 has been recognized for its rapid growth and has been listed among the fastest-growing companies in the consulting sector (Logic20/20, 2024).

Company Culture

Logic20/20 promotes a collaborative and inclusive culture, known as the "Culture of We," which emphasizes teamwork, transparency, and community engagement. The company has been recognized multiple times as one of Washington's Best Companies to Work For (Logic20/20, 2024).
