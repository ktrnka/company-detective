About Logic20/20

Logic20/20 is a business and technology consulting firm founded in 2005 by Christian O’Meara. The company is headquartered in Seattle, Washington, USA (Crunchbase, 2025). Logic20/20 specializes in enhancing client performance through strategy, technology, and data, offering services such as advanced analytics, digital transformation, and strategy & operations (Logic20/20, Company Overview).

The company serves a diverse range of industries, including energy and utilities, technology and communications, financial services, healthcare, and education. It operates primarily in a B2B model, working with clients ranging from startups to Fortune 100 companies, as well as state and local government organizations (Logic20/20, About Logic20/20).

Logic20/20 has been recognized for its workplace culture, being named a "Best Company to Work For" multiple times and achieving certification from Great Place To Work based on positive employee feedback (Great Place To Work, Working at Logic20/20). The company emphasizes a collaborative culture, continuous learning, and core values of excellence, integrity, and teamwork (Logic20/20, Company Overview).

The firm has also been recognized for its growth, being listed as one of Inc. Magazine's fastest-growing firms (Logic20/20, About Logic20/20). Logic20/20's revenue and specific financial details are not publicly disclosed, but its recognition for growth suggests a strong market position.

Key Personnel

  • Christian O’Meara: Co-founder and Chief Executive Officer, focused on strategic business development and fostering a positive workplace culture (Logic20/20, Company Overview).
  • Travis Jones: Chief Operating Officer, responsible for growth initiatives and client enablement, with extensive international consulting experience (Logic20/20, Company Overview).
  • Julie Meredith: Chief Financial Officer, overseeing finance and accounting operations, with a strong background in financial strategy (LinkedIn, 2024-03-14).
  • Will Schmidt: General Manager, Head of Consulting, leading the consulting practice and ensuring client satisfaction (Logic20/20, Company Overview).
  • Nick Maddock: Managing Director, Strategy & Operations, focusing on consulting innovation and project delivery excellence (Logic20/20, Company Overview).

Product and Service Innovations

Renewable Energy Forecasting

Logic20/20 has developed AI and ML-powered forecasting solutions to improve the accuracy of renewable energy forecasts, helping utilities maintain a stable energy supply and meet carbon-free electricity goals (Lago, Logic20/20, 2024-02-20).

AI in Customer Service

The company is innovating in customer service by implementing AI tools that enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of customer service operations, transforming call centers into revenue-generating assets (Parker, Lee, Logic20/20, 2024-05-10).

Partnerships

Logic20/20 has partnered with Optelos to deliver AI analysis for drone inspections in the utility sector, combining technical expertise with visual data management capabilities to automate inspection programs (Bauman, Optelos, 2021-02-09).

Thought Leadership

The company has released a white paper on the transformative power of asset image analytics, showcasing its commitment to thought leadership and innovation in the consulting industry (Mike Cohen, 2024-09-30).
