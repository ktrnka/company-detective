About Logic20/20

Logic20/20 is a Seattle-based business and technology consulting firm founded on January 1, 2005, by Christian O’Meara (Crunchbase, 2024). The company specializes in providing consulting services that focus on strategy, technology, and data to enhance client performance. Their services include advanced analytics, digital transformation, and strategy & operations, catering to a wide range of industries such as energy & utilities, technology & communications, financial services, life sciences, healthcare, government, education, insurance, and media & entertainment (Logic20/20 Company Overview, 2024).

As of December 31, 2022, Logic20/20 employed 221 individuals, marking a 10.2% decrease from the previous year (Tracxn, 2023-01-22). The company operates without external funding, having not raised any funding rounds to date (Tracxn, 2023-01-22). Despite this, Logic20/20 has been recognized multiple times as one of Washington's Best Companies to Work For and has been listed among Inc. Magazine's fastest-growing firms (Tracxn, 2023-01-22).

Logic20/20's business model is primarily B2B, serving a diverse clientele across various sectors. The company collaborates with leading technology providers to enhance its service offerings and deliver tailored solutions to clients (Logic20/20 Company Overview, 2024).

They focus on overcoming common roadblocks to AI implementation, such as employee resistance, data gaps, and skill shortages. Logic20/20 emphasizes a strong company culture, which is reflected in their recognition as a "Best Company to Work For." The company also offers competitive compensation packages and various employee benefits, including paid time off and community engagement opportunities (Senior Consultant - Data Privacy, 2024).

The firm operates in a competitive landscape, with notable competitors including UST, Globant, and Persistent, which have raised significant funding and have larger employee bases (Tracxn, 2023-01-22).
