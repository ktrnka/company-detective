About Logic20/20

Logic20/20 is a business and technology consulting firm headquartered in Seattle, Washington. The company was founded on January 1, 2005, and has since developed a reputation for helping clients address complex challenges through expertise in advanced analytics, digital transformation, grid operations, and strategy and operations (Crunchbase, 2024). The firm operates primarily in the B2B sector, partnering with organizations such as T-Mobile to improve data accessibility and program evaluation (Logic20/20, 2024).

The company offers a range of consulting services, including:

  • Advanced Analytics: Utilizing data to drive insights and decision-making.
  • Digital Transformation: Assisting organizations in adapting to the evolving digital landscape.
  • Strategy & Operations: Developing strategies to optimize operations and achieve business goals.

Logic20/20 is known for delivering high-impact solutions tailored to client needs, with notable projects including operationalizing machine learning intelligence for a major utility and building a data foundation to comply with new regulations (Logic20/20, 2024).

The firm has been recognized multiple times as one of the best companies to work for, including being named an eight-time "Best Company to Work For" and ranking seventh in the 2024 Fortune Best Workplaces in Consulting & Professional Services (Cohen, EIN Presswire, 2024-09-18). Logic20/20 promotes a "Culture of We," emphasizing teamwork, transparency, and community engagement (Logic20/20, 2024).

While specific revenue figures are not disclosed, Logic20/20 has been recognized as one of the fastest-growing companies in the consulting sector (Senior Dynamics 365 Developer, Logic20/20, Inc.).

Product Developments

Renewable Energy Forecasting

Logic20/20 is developing AI- and machine learning-powered forecasting solutions to help utilities manage the intermittent nature of renewable energy sources. This initiative supports the U.S. government's goals for carbon-free electricity (Lago, Logic20/20, 2024-02-20).

Customer Service AI Solutions

The firm is enhancing customer service operations through AI applications that empower customer service representatives, transforming customer service from a cost center into a revenue-generating asset (Lee, Logic20/20, 2024-05-10).

Partnerships

Logic20/20 has partnered with Optelos to deliver drone inspection services combined with AI analysis for utilities, aiming to automate inspection programs and improve speed, safety, and cost-effectiveness (Bauman, Optelos, 2021-02-09).
