About Logic20/20

Logic20/20 is a business and technology consulting firm founded on January 1, 2005, and headquartered in Seattle, Washington, USA (Crunchbase, 2025). The company specializes in simplifying complex projects at the intersection of business and technology, focusing on strategy, technology, and data to enhance client performance (Logic20/20 Company Overview).

Services

Logic20/20 offers a comprehensive range of consulting services across several domains:

  • Advanced Analytics: This includes data science, predictive analytics, AI and machine learning, visual analytics, data integration and transformation, data migration, and data privacy.
  • Digital Transformation: Services include packaged software solutions, digital and cloud strategy, architecture, UX/UI design and implementation, and technical project/product management.
  • Strategy & Operations: This encompasses corporate and business strategy, business process optimization, IT adoption, organizational transformation, product strategy and operations, and PMO design and operations (Logic20/20 Company Overview).

Products

The company specializes in creating tailored solutions that leverage technology to improve communication, collaboration, and customer experience. They focus on delivering high-performing analytics and digital transformation strategies (Logic20/20 Company Overview).

Customers

Logic20/20 serves a diverse range of industries, including energy and utilities, technology and communications, financial services, life sciences, healthcare, government, education, insurance, and media and entertainment. Notable clients include T-Mobile and various educational institutions (Logic20/20 Company Overview).

Financial and Operational Scale

While specific revenue figures are not publicly available, Logic20/20 has been recognized for its rapid growth, being listed among Inc. Magazine's fastest-growing firms (Change Manager - Logic20/20). The company employs a diverse team of professionals, although exact employee numbers are not disclosed.

Company Culture

Logic20/20 fosters a culture of learning, growth, and collaboration, emphasizing mentorship and career management. The firm values transparency and communication, aiming to create high-impact solutions through teamwork (Logic20/20 Company Overview). The company has been recognized multiple times as one of the best companies to work for, being named a "Best Company to Work For" nine times and one of Washington's Best Companies to Work For for five consecutive years (Logic20/20 on 2024-07-18).
