About Logic20/20

Logic20/20 is a business and technology consulting firm founded on January 1, 2005, and headquartered in Seattle, Washington, USA (Crunchbase, 2025). The company specializes in enhancing client performance through strategy, technology, and data, with a focus on simplifying complex projects and helping customers predict and increase sales (Logic20/20, 2023).

Services and Products

Logic20/20 offers a range of consulting services, including:

  • Advanced Analytics: Utilizing data to drive business decisions.
  • Digital Transformation: Assisting organizations in adapting to the digital landscape.
  • Strategy & Operations: Developing strategies to optimize operations and achieve business goals (Logic20/20, 2023).

The company also provides tailored solutions such as end-to-end data strategy, responsible AI implementation, and digital transformation trends analysis (Logic20/20, 2023).

Customers and Market

Logic20/20 serves a diverse client base, including startups and Fortune 100 companies, with notable clients like T-Mobile and Microsoft (builtin, 2023). The company operates primarily in a B2B model, focusing on enhancing efficiency, coordination, and velocity through its services (Logic20/20, 2023).

Company Scale and Financial Performance

While specific revenue figures are not disclosed, Logic20/20 has been recognized for its rapid growth and has been listed among the fastest-growing firms by Inc. Magazine (builtin, 2023). The company employs a diverse team of professionals working on various projects across different sectors, indicating a robust operational scale.

Company Culture

Logic20/20 promotes a culture of unity and community engagement, encouraging employees to dedicate their time and talents to create a better tomorrow. The company values learning, connection, and high-impact solutions (Logic20/20, 2023). It has been recognized as one of Washington's Best Companies to Work For for nine consecutive years (EINPresswire, 2025-01-13).
