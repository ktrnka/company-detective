About Quartet Health

Quartet Health is a value-based behavioral healthcare company founded on January 1, 2014, and headquartered in New York, United States (Crunchbase, 2024). The company focuses on expanding access to high-quality care for individuals with behavioral health needs, including serious mental illness (SMI). Quartet Health operates in 39 states and partners with health plans, health systems, and community mental health centers to provide comprehensive behavioral health solutions (Quartet Health on 2024-09-20).

Quartet Health's services include care enablement and delivery, where they offer end-to-end behavioral health solutions that identify, engage, and care for patients. They collaborate with various health plans and systems to connect patients with high-quality providers, including their own medical group (Quartet Health on 2024-09-20).

The company primarily operates in a B2B model, serving clients such as health plans, health systems, and community mental health centers (Quartet Health on 2024-09-20). Quartet Health is backed by prominent investors, including Oak HC/FT, GV (formerly Google Ventures), F-Prime Capital Partners, and Centene Corporation, which indicates strong financial support and confidence in its business model (Built In NYC, 2023).

Quartet Health has raised a total of $265 million in funding, with the most recent round being $51 million on February 17, 2022 (Crunchbase, 2024).

Key Personnel

  • Christina Mainelli: CEO of Quartet Health. She has emphasized the importance of integrated care, particularly for individuals with SMI, highlighting the interconnectedness of physical and mental health needs (Quartet Health, 2024-03-19).

Recent Developments

  • Whole Health Program Launch: On March 19, 2024, Quartet Health announced the launch of its Whole Health program in partnership with Clover Health. This program is designed to provide a comprehensive care delivery solution for patients with SMI by integrating physical and behavioral health services. The initiative aims to address the fragmented healthcare system that often leads to poor outcomes for these patients (Quartet Health, 2024-03-19).

  • Partnership with Arbital Health: Quartet Health partnered with Arbital Health to increase value-based mental healthcare, as reported on October 17, 2024 (Mobi Health News, 2024-10-17).
