About Quartet Health

Quartet Health, founded in 2014 and headquartered in New York City, is a tech-enabled behavioral health company that integrates behavioral and medical health services. The company operates a population health management platform designed to connect patients with mental health providers, thereby improving access to care and health outcomes for individuals with serious mental illnesses (SMI) (Tracxn, 2021).

Quartet Health partners with health plans, health systems, and community mental health centers across 39 states to provide comprehensive behavioral health solutions. Their services include therapy, psychiatry, and digital options for self-management of mental health symptoms. The company emphasizes value-based care, aiming to reduce healthcare costs and improve patient outcomes (Quartet Health, 2024).

Quartet Health generates revenue primarily through partnerships with healthcare providers and payers, operating in a B2B model. Notable partners include Clover Health, with whom they launched the Whole Health program to provide integrated care for seniors with SMI (MobiHealthNews, 2024).

The company has raised $222 million in funding from investors such as Google Ventures and Centene Corporation (Tracxn, 2021). Quartet Health serves approximately 21 million patients through its platform, which connects them to over 10,000 behavioral health providers (Mainelli, 2024).

Quartet Health is recognized for its innovative approach to integrating behavioral and physical health services, ranking second among competitors like SonderMind and aptihealth (Tracxn, 2021).

Key Personnel

  • Christina Mainelli: CEO of Quartet Health. Mainelli has been instrumental in driving the company's mission to address the fragmented healthcare system for patients with SMI. She emphasizes the importance of holistic care that integrates mental and physical health services (Quartet Health, 2024).

  • Karen Mitchell: Chief Growth Officer, appointed in March 2024. Mitchell brings extensive experience in healthcare sales, expected to enhance Quartet's expansion efforts and service offerings (Lovett, 2024).

News

Partnerships and Programs

In March 2024, Quartet Health partnered with Clover Health to launch the Whole Health program, focusing on comprehensive care for seniors with SMI. This initiative integrates physical and behavioral health services, offering both in-person and virtual therapy options (MobiHealthNews, 2024).

Acquisitions

In December 2021, Quartet Health acquired InnovaTel Telepsychiatry, expanding its virtual mental health service capabilities. This acquisition followed a $60 million funding round aimed at enhancing patient identification tools and supporting additional health conditions (MobiHealthNews, 2024).

Leadership Changes

In March 2024, Quartet appointed Karen Mitchell as Chief Growth Officer to support the company's growth and service expansion (Lovett, 2024).
