About Quartet Health

Quartet Health is a value-based behavioral healthcare company founded on January 1, 2014, and headquartered in New York City. The company focuses on integrating behavioral and medical health through its Population Health Management platform, which facilitates communication and collaboration between medical and behavioral health providers. This approach aims to improve patient care quality by addressing gaps in care and enhancing network quality driven by behavioral and medical comorbidities (Tracxn, 2021-08-17).

Quartet Health operates in 39 states and partners with health plans, health systems, and community mental health centers to provide comprehensive behavioral health solutions. The company offers services such as therapy, psychiatry, and digital options to improve mental health care access and delivery (Quartet Health). Quartet Health's business model is primarily B2B, collaborating with various health plans and systems to streamline mental health care access (Health Systems).

The company has raised a total of $265 million across multiple funding rounds, with notable investors including Google Ventures, Polaris Partners, and Centene Corporation (Crunchbase, 2024). Quartet Health's recent financial performance and strategic partnerships, such as the one with Clover Health, position it as a leader in the behavioral health space (Tracxn, 2021-08-17).

Key Personnel

  • Puneet Singh: CEO of Quartet Health. He has a robust background in healthcare, having previously held the position of Chief Growth Officer at Anthem and played a significant role in the growth of Aspire Health. His experience includes private equity investment and corporate strategy in healthcare, starting his career as an investment banker at Goldman Sachs (About Us).

News

Recent Developments

New Product Launch

On March 19, 2024, Quartet Health announced the launch of its Whole Health program in partnership with Clover Health. This program aims to provide a comprehensive care delivery solution for patients with serious mental illnesses by integrating physical and behavioral health services. The program offers 24/7 care access, medication support, and care coordination (Quartet Health Launches Innovative Whole Health Program, 2024-03-19).

Acquisition

In 2021, Quartet Health acquired innovaTel, a telepsychiatry provider, to enhance its service offerings and improve access to mental health care (Remote Quartet Health/Customer Support Specialist).

Partnerships

Quartet Health's partnership with Clover Health is a significant development, allowing the company to leverage Clover's resources to better serve underserved populations with serious mental illnesses. This collaboration aims to create a more integrated healthcare experience for patients (Quartet Health Launches Innovative Whole Health Program, 2024-03-19).

Financial Performance and Scale

Quartet Health has raised a total of $265 million, reflecting strong investor confidence in its business model and growth potential. The company ranks second among 43 active competitors in the behavioral health sector (Tracxn, 2021-08-17).

Employee Insights and Culture

Quartet Health promotes a collaborative and innovative work environment, emphasizing a patient-first approach. The company is committed to diversity and inclusion, actively encouraging applicants from various backgrounds to apply for positions (Senior Actuarial Analyst - Quartet Health).

Conclusion

Quartet Health is making significant strides in the behavioral healthcare sector through innovative programs and strategic partnerships. The recent launch of the Whole Health program and the acquisition of innovaTel position the company well to address the complex needs of patients with serious mental illnesses. With strong financial backing and a commitment to integrated care, Quartet Health is poised for continued growth and impact in the healthcare landscape.
