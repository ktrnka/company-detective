About Quartet Health

Quartet Health is a behavioral healthcare company founded on January 1, 2014, and is based in New York, United States (Crunchbase, 2025). The company focuses on improving access to high-quality mental health care through a tech-enabled platform that connects patients with appropriate care providers. Quartet Health partners with health plans, health systems, and community mental health centers across 39 states in the U.S. to deliver comprehensive behavioral health solutions (Quartet Health on 2024-09-20).

Quartet Health operates primarily in the B2B space, serving clients such as health plans, health systems, and community mental health centers (Quartet Health on 2024-09-20). The company generates revenue by providing these organizations with a platform that facilitates the identification, engagement, and care of patients with behavioral health needs. Quartet Health's services include psychiatric evaluations, therapy, medication management, and patient education, enhanced by its acquisition of innovaTel, a telepsychiatry provider, in 2021 (Tracxn, 2021-08-17).

The company has raised a total of $265 million in funding, with the most recent round being $51 million on February 17, 2022 (Crunchbase, 2025). Quartet Health's scale is significant, as it is described as a leading company in the behavioral health space, although specific employee numbers are not detailed (Tracxn, 2021-08-17).

Quartet Health's products are distributed through partnerships with health plans and systems, which help connect patients with high-quality providers, including Quartet's own medical group (Quartet Health on 2024-09-20). The company is committed to diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) within its workforce, fostering a culture that reflects these values (Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion at Quartet on 2021-10-18).

Key Personnel

Recent Developments

  • Whole Health Program Launch: On March 19, 2024, Quartet Health launched its Whole Health program in partnership with Clover Health. This program aims to provide comprehensive care for patients with serious mental illnesses by integrating physical and mental health services (Quartet Health, 2024-03-19). The program is designed to offer coordinated care, including 24/7 access, medication support, and local community resources (Quartet Health, 2024-03-19).
