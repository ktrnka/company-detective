About Quartet Health

Quartet Health is a behavioral healthcare company founded on January 1, 2014, and is based in New York, United States (Crunchbase, 2025). The company focuses on improving access to high-quality mental health care through a value-based approach. Quartet Health operates in 39 states and partners with health plans, health systems, and community mental health centers to provide comprehensive behavioral health solutions (Quartet Health, 2023).

Quartet Health's primary product is its behavioral health care delivery system, which integrates various services to ensure patients receive the necessary support. This includes partnerships with digital care providers like Brightside, which offers online mental health services such as personalized medication prescribing and therapy (Quartet Health, 2023).

The company primarily operates in a B2B model, serving health plans, health systems, and community mental health centers. Quartet Health generates revenue by partnering with these entities to enhance access to behavioral health care (Tracxn, 2021-08-17). The company has raised a total of $265 million in funding, with the most recent round being $51 million on February 17, 2022 (Crunchbase, 2025).

Quartet Health has undergone significant changes over time, including the acquisition of innovaTel in 2021 to expand its capabilities in behavioral health staffing solutions (Built In NYC, 2021). In January 2025, Quartet Health was acquired by NeuroFlow, a company that integrates behavioral health into physical health workflows through its AI-powered platform (NeuroFlow, 2025).

Key Personnel

Christina Mainelli serves as the CEO of Quartet Health. She has emphasized the mission-driven nature of the company, focusing on putting patients and customers first (NeuroFlow, 2025). Specific details about other key personnel were not provided in the available content.

News

Acquisition by NeuroFlow

On January 29, 2025, Quartet Health was acquired by NeuroFlow. This acquisition is expected to enhance NeuroFlow's referral and care navigation capabilities for payers, health systems, and the federal government. NeuroFlow's CEO, Chris Molaro, expressed enthusiasm about the acquisition, highlighting the potential to make behavioral health care more accessible, personalized, and effective (NeuroFlow, 2025).

Partnerships and Collaborations

Quartet Health has established partnerships with various health plans and systems, including Independence Blue Cross. Dr. Richard Snyder, Executive Vice President at Independence Blue Cross, expressed pride in the collaboration with Quartet and excitement about the new chapter with NeuroFlow (NeuroFlow, 2025).

In October 2024, Quartet Health partnered with Arbital Health to increase value-based mental healthcare (Mobi Health News, 2024-10-17). Earlier in 2024, Quartet Health collaborated with Clover Health to launch a whole health program addressing mental illness in seniors (MobiHealthNews, 2024-04-03).

Product Development and Market Position

Quartet Health's primary product offering is its behavioral health care enablement platform, designed to streamline the integration of behavioral health services into existing healthcare workflows (Tracxn, 2021-08-17). The acquisition by NeuroFlow positions Quartet Health to leverage enhanced technology and analytics capabilities, potentially improving the quality and accessibility of behavioral health care across the U.S. (NeuroFlow, 2025).

Recent Developments

In January 2025, Iris Telehealth acquired innovaTel from Quartet Health to enhance telepsychiatry services (Chris Larson, 2025-01-21). This move is part of a broader strategy to address workforce challenges in behavioral health (Built In NYC, 2021).

Quartet Health has been featured in various market reports, including the Digital Health Coaching Market Report 2025-2030, which profiles leading companies in the sector (Research and Markets, 2025-03-20).
