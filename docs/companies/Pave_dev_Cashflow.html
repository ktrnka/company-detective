About Pave.dev

Pave.dev is a fintech company that was founded with the mission of revolutionizing financial services through cashflow analytics. The company focuses on enhancing credit decision-making and expanding financial inclusion, particularly for underserved consumers and small businesses. Pave.dev's primary product, Cashflow, leverages real-time data analysis to provide insights into a borrower's financial health, which helps lenders make more informed credit decisions.

Pave.dev operates in the B2B space, targeting lenders who can benefit from improved risk assessment and credit decisioning processes. The company's offerings include Cashflow Scores and Cashflow Attributes, which provide a detailed view of a borrower's financial stability and ability to repay loans. These tools are particularly useful for personal loans, small dollar loans, and credit and charge cards.

The company promotes a remote work culture and values diversity and mission-driven individuals. Pave.dev emphasizes teamwork and innovation in delivering financial solutions. While specific revenue figures and employee counts are not disclosed, the company is positioned within a rapidly expanding market, with the U.S. fleet card market reaching $93.5 billion in 2023 (Chikota, 2023-11-24).

Pave.dev has recently partnered with Taktile to integrate its cashflow analytics into Taktile's decision engine, enhancing credit underwriting for small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) (Taktile, 2024-08-01).

Key Personnel

Ema Rouf is a co-founder of Pave.dev and has expressed enthusiasm about the company's partnership with Taktile. She highlights the alignment of Taktile's innovative solutions with Pave.dev's mission to provide superior cashflow analytics for business lenders (Taktile, 2024-08-01).

Partnership with Taktile

On August 1, 2024, Pave.dev announced a partnership with Taktile to automate SMB credit underwriting. This collaboration integrates Pave.dev's cashflow analytics into Taktile's decision engine, allowing lenders to streamline their workflows and improve decision-making processes (Taktile, 2024-08-01).

Launch of New Cashflow Products

Pave.dev recently launched a suite of cashflow products designed to help SMB lenders validate cashflows, underwrite loans, and assign credit limits using real-time cashflow analytics. This initiative aims to address the needs of underserved small businesses and enhance their access to credit (Taktile, 2024-08-01).
