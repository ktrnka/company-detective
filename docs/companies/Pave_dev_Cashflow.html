About Pave.dev

Pave.dev is a technology company dedicated to enhancing financial inclusion for underserved consumers and businesses. The company was founded with the mission to empower lenders to convert underserved applicants into successful customers by providing tools that allow lenders to assess affordability, reduce defaults, and boost approvals through cashflow analytics. Pave operates with a fully remote team, valuing self-starters who are passionate about redefining creditworthiness in the financial ecosystem (Pave Overview, Pave.dev).

Products and Services

Pave offers a range of services and products designed to improve credit risk assessment and lending strategies:

  • Cashflow Analytics: Tools for lenders to analyze borrower cashflows, helping to increase approvals and reduce defaults across various credit products, including cash advances and personal loans.
  • Cashflow Scores: Predictive scores that assess a borrower's likelihood of making timely payments on credit products, enabling lenders to adjust their strategies in real-time.
  • Cashflow Attributes: A set of attributes that provide insights into a borrower's stability, affordability, assets, and willingness to repay, enhancing risk models and approval rates.
  • Flexible Rent Score: A score designed for property management that helps assess tenant risk and offers personalized payment options, reducing defaults and expanding approvals.
  • Fuel Charge Card Score: A score tailored for fleet card providers to assess repayment risk and optimize credit limits based on a business's cashflow.
  • Cash Advance Score: A score that predicts a user's ability to repay cash advances, allowing lenders to minimize defaults and increase approval rates.
  • Payroll Prediction Model: A model that predicts payroll transactions, helping users manage their finances better and align repayment schedules with income dates (Pave Overview, Pave.dev).

Customers and Market

Pave serves a variety of financial institutions and lenders, including those in the consumer credit, cash advance, and fleet card sectors. Their solutions are designed to help these businesses better assess risk and improve their lending processes (Pave Overview, Pave.dev).
