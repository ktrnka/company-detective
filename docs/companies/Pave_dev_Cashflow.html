About Pave.dev

Pave.dev, also known as Pave Financial Inc., is a fintech company founded on January 1, 2020 (Crunchbase, 2024). The company specializes in enhancing credit risk assessment through cashflow intelligence, aiming to increase access to credit for underserved borrowers by utilizing AI-powered cashflow analytics and scores (Ualett, PR Newswire, 2024). Pave operates entirely remotely, fostering a diverse and mission-driven team (Power credit decisions with cashflow intelligence).

Pave's primary product, Cashflow, leverages real-time bank transaction data to provide insights into consumers' financial health, enhancing credit risk models by offering a more nuanced understanding of borrowers' cashflow patterns (Ualett, PR Newswire, 2024). The company offers services such as Cashflow Scores and Data Integration, which help predict a borrower's likelihood and ability to repay loans by analyzing real-time cashflow data, payment history, and other financial attributes (Power credit decisions with cashflow intelligence).

Pave's clientele includes lenders looking to unlock new customer segments without increasing risk, particularly focusing on underserved yet creditworthy borrowers who may be overlooked by traditional risk assessments (Ualett, PR Newswire, 2024). The company is positioned within a rapidly growing market, especially as the gig economy expands, with over 70 million Americans participating (Ualett, PR Newswire, 2024).

Pave has raised a total of $6 million in funding as of July 1, 2023 (Crunchbase, 2024). The company promotes a culture of remote work, diversity and inclusion, and continuous improvement, emphasizing flexibility and the ability to work from anywhere (Power credit decisions with cashflow intelligence).

Key Personnel

Ema Rouf is a Co-founder and President of Pave. She has been vocal about the company's mission to expand access to funding for underserved and non-traditional borrowers, emphasizing the importance of personalized financial solutions through real-time cashflow insights (Ualett, PR Newswire, 2024).

Partnership with Ualett

On October 28, 2024, Pave announced a strategic partnership with Ualett, a mobile app solution for cash advances to gig workers. This collaboration aims to automate and personalize cash advance experiences tailored to the income patterns of independent contractors. The integration of Pave's real-time Cash Advance Score allows Ualett to offer personalized financial solutions, potentially reducing default rates by up to 50% (Ualett, PR Newswire, 2024).

Focus on Gig Economy

The partnership with Ualett aligns with the growing gig economy, which is projected to encompass 50% of the U.S. workforce by 2027. This strategic move positions Pave to capitalize on the increasing demand for flexible financial solutions among gig workers (Ualett, PR Newswire, 2024).
