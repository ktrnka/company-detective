About Pave.dev

Pave.dev is a company focused on enhancing financial inclusion for underserved consumers and businesses in the United States. The company addresses the challenge faced by over 100 million individuals and businesses who lack access to equitable credit due to traditional financial systems not recognizing their data. Pave's mission is to enable developers to create personalized financial experiences with minimal friction and cost.

Company History

Pave was founded with the vision of redefining creditworthiness and expanding financial inclusion. The company operates with a diverse and fully remote team, emphasizing the importance of talented self-starters who are passionate about its mission.

Services and Products

Pave offers a suite of tools and analytics designed to assist lenders in making informed decisions:

  • Cashflow Analytics: These tools help lenders assess affordability, reduce defaults, and increase approvals for various credit products, including cash advances and personal loans.
  • Cashflow Scores: Predictive scores that assess a borrower's likelihood of making initial payments on credit products, enabling lenders to adjust strategies in real-time and safely increase credit limits.
  • Cashflow Attributes: Instant generation of attributes that provide insights into a borrower's stability, affordability, assets, and willingness, helping lenders make informed decisions.
  • Collections Whitepaper: A resource exploring how AI and machine learning can enhance collections processes and improve recovery rates through personalized payment plans.

Target Customers

Pave primarily serves lenders, including consumer credit providers and fleet card providers, by equipping them with advanced analytics and real-time data to enhance risk management and promote growth.

Recent Developments

Partnership with Ualett

On October 28, 2024, Pave announced a strategic partnership with Ualett, a mobile app solution for cash advances to gig workers. This collaboration aims to automate and personalize financial solutions for gig workers by integrating Pave's real-time Cash Advance Score and Attributes into Ualett's platform. The partnership is expected to enhance Ualett's ability to scale its services while maintaining a personalized user experience (Ualett, PR Newswire, 2024-10-28).

Executive Insights

  • Ricky Michel Presbot, CEO of Ualett: Highlighted the importance of the partnership with Pave, stating that Pave's advanced cashflow analytics allow Ualett to offer personalized cash advances tailored to each worker's income, with the potential to significantly reduce default rates (Ualett, PR Newswire, 2024-10-28).
