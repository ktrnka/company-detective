About Osano

Osano, founded in 2018 and headquartered in Austin, Texas, is a prominent provider of data privacy management solutions. The company focuses on simplifying data privacy compliance for organizations, helping them build, manage, and scale effective privacy programs. Osano is recognized as a B Corporation, which underscores its commitment to social and environmental performance, accountability, and transparency (PR Newswire, 2024-06-11).

Services and Products

Osano offers a comprehensive suite of services designed to address various aspects of data privacy compliance, including:

  • Consent Management: Tools to manage user consent for data processing and cookie usage.
  • Data Subject Rights Management: Automation for handling requests from individuals regarding their personal data.
  • Data Mapping: Solutions to help organizations understand and manage their data flows.
  • Privacy Assessments: Regular assessments to ensure compliance with privacy laws.
  • Vendor Risk Management: Tools to evaluate and monitor the privacy practices of third-party vendors.

The platform supports compliance with major regulations such as GDPR and CCPA, providing businesses with the tools they need to protect personal data and maintain customer trust (Osano, 2023).

Customers and Market Position

Osano serves over 5,000 organizations, including notable companies like Lattice, TBG Security, Demio, Herschend Family Entertainment, Reshoevn8r, and Autorola. These customers utilize Osano's platform to streamline their privacy operations and ensure compliance with data protection laws (Osano, 2023). The company is actively exploring additional acquisitions to further expand its platform and global reach (PR Newswire, 2023-12-05).

Revenue and Distribution

While specific revenue figures are not disclosed, Osano's business model is primarily B2B, focusing on providing privacy management solutions to organizations. The company's products are distributed through a SaaS model, allowing customers to access and manage their privacy compliance needs online.

Company Culture

Osano promotes a culture centered around data privacy and compliance, emphasizing ethical data handling practices. The company has been recognized for its positive workplace culture, being named a "Best Place to Work" by Fortune and Great Place to Work in 2024 (PR Newswire, 2024-06-11).

Key Personnel

  • Arlo Gilbert: CEO and Co-Founder of Osano, with over 25 years of experience in building technology startups across various industries. His leadership focuses on fostering a culture of transparency and trust in data privacy (Osano, 2023).

  • Ryan Macia: Appointed as the Chief Financial Officer (CFO) in June 2024. Macia brings extensive experience in guiding high-growth technology companies and is expected to play a crucial role in Osano's ongoing expansion and acquisition strategy (PR Newswire, 2024-06-25).

News

Recent Acquisitions

On December 5, 2023, Osano announced the acquisition of WireWheel, an enterprise provider of data privacy solutions. This strategic acquisition is expected to enhance Osano's platform by integrating WireWheel's industry-leading privacy assessment tools, which automate auditing processes, identify privacy risks, and create necessary regulatory documentation (PR Newswire, 2023-12-05).

Product Developments

In April 2024, Osano launched a comprehensive data privacy management platform designed to automate and simplify global compliance processes. Key features include cookie consent management, a unified consent and preference hub, data mapping, subject rights management, and vendor privacy risk management (PR Newswire, 2024-04-02).

Leadership Changes

In June 2024, Osano appointed Ryan Macia as the new CFO, highlighting the company's focus on strategic growth and acquisitions (PR Newswire, 2024-06-25).

Conclusion

Osano is positioned for significant growth in the data privacy management market, bolstered by its recent acquisition of WireWheel and the launch of its comprehensive platform. With a strong leadership team and a commitment to workplace culture, Osano is well-equipped to navigate the complexities of data privacy compliance and support its expanding global customer base.
