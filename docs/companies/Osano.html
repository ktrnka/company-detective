About Osano

Osano is a data privacy management platform founded in 2018 and headquartered in Austin, Texas. The company is recognized as a B Corporation, which indicates a commitment to social and environmental performance, accountability, and transparency. Osano's primary product is its data privacy platform, which helps organizations manage and scale their privacy programs effectively. The platform focuses on compliance with various data privacy laws, including GDPR and CCPA, and offers tools for consent management, data subject rights, data mapping, assessments, and vendor risk management (Osano Website).

Osano operates primarily in the B2B sector, serving over 5,000 organizations. Its customers include companies like Lattice, TBG Security, and Demio, which have integrated Osano into their operations to enhance privacy management and compliance (Osano Customer Stories). The company generates revenue through subscription-based pricing plans tailored to different organizational needs, ensuring accessibility for startups and non-profits (Osano Plans).

The company has been recognized for its workplace culture, being named a "Best Place to Work" by Fortune and Great Place To Work in 2024 (PR Newswire, 2024-06-11). Osano's mission is to help businesses build, manage, and scale effective data privacy programs, thereby increasing trust and ensuring compliance with various data privacy regulations (PR Newswire, 2024-04-02).

Key Personnel

Arlo Gilbert is the co-founder and CEO of Osano. He has been instrumental in leading the company towards simplifying data privacy for businesses. Gilbert has emphasized the importance of creating a user-friendly platform that addresses the complexities of data privacy management. His leadership has been pivotal in fostering a culture that prioritizes customer experience and operational excellence (PR Newswire, 2024-04-02).

News

Recent Acquisition

On December 5, 2023, Osano announced the acquisition of WireWheel, an enterprise provider of data privacy solutions. This strategic move aims to enhance Osano's platform by integrating WireWheel's industry-leading privacy assessment tools, which are designed to support large-scale data privacy programs (PR Newswire, 2023-12-05). CEO Arlo Gilbert highlighted the complementary nature of the two companies' solutions, stating that the acquisition is high-impact for both Osano and WireWheel’s customers (PR Newswire, 2023-12-05).

Product Developments

In April 2024, Osano launched a comprehensive data privacy management platform that further simplifies global compliance and privacy operations. This platform includes features like a Unified Consent and Preference Hub, automated data mapping, and streamlined subject rights management (PR Newswire, 2024-04-02). The integration of WireWheel's capabilities will allow Osano to offer enhanced features such as automated auditing, privacy risk identification, and regulatory documentation creation (PR Newswire, 2023-12-05).

Company Scale and Market Position

Osano has established itself as a leader in the data privacy management sector, with a growing customer base that includes over 5,000 organizations. The company is recognized for its user-friendly platform and has been acknowledged as a leader in consent management by industry analysts (PR Newswire, 2024-04-02). The acquisition of WireWheel is expected to solidify Osano's position in the market and expand its global footprint, as the company is actively exploring additional acquisition opportunities (PR Newswire, 2023-12-05).

Employee and Workplace Culture

Osano has been recognized for its positive workplace culture, being named one of the "Best Workplaces in Texas" for two consecutive years. This recognition is based on employee feedback regarding leadership accessibility, support for well-being, and equitable pay (PR Newswire, 2024-06-11). Gilbert attributes this success to the company's core values of bold action, authenticity, inclusion, operational excellence, and a delightful experience for employees and customers alike (PR Newswire, 2024-06-11).
