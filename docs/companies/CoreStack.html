About CoreStack

CoreStack, founded on November 4, 2016, is a cloud management platform headquartered in Bellevue, Washington. The company specializes in AI-powered multi-cloud governance solutions, aiming to help enterprises achieve continuous and autonomous cloud governance. CoreStack's platform is designed to enhance performance and flexibility for organizations managing cloud services, ensuring compliance and automating cloud operations (Crunchbase, 2024).

CoreStack's flagship product, CR360™, offers a comprehensive view of cloud resources across various providers, including AWS, Azure, GCP, and OCI. The platform integrates capabilities such as FinOps, SecOps, and CloudOps, allowing enterprises to optimize costs, ensure compliance, and enhance operational efficiencies (CoreStack, 2024-07-04).

The company operates in a B2B model, serving a diverse range of global enterprises across multiple industries. CoreStack has established strategic partnerships with major cloud providers like Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure, and Amazon AWS, which enhances its credibility and market presence (CoreStack Company Overview, 2021-08-17).

CoreStack has raised a total of $45 million in funding, with notable rounds including $30 million on November 24, 2021, and $8 million on March 17, 2020 (Crunchbase, 2024). The company has been recognized in the industry, being named an IDC Innovator for Cloud Management Solutions and featured in the Gartner Magic Quadrant for Cloud Management Platforms in 2020 (CoreStack, 2024-07-04).

Key Personnel

CoreStack's leadership team comprises industry veterans with extensive experience in enterprise software and IT infrastructure. Many of the team members previously worked at Mindtree, which was instrumental in the development of Microsoft Azure (Puskoor, 2020-03-30). The company has recently strengthened its leadership with the addition of a Chief Revenue Officer and a Chief Marketing Officer, both of whom have extensive experience in growth companies and successful exits (Puskoor, 2020-03-30).

Recent Developments

  • Strategic Partnerships: CoreStack announced a strategic partnership with ConRes to launch ConRes Insights for Multi-Cloud Optimization on October 21, 2024 (Business Wire, 2024-10-21). Additionally, CoreStack partnered with ZainTech to boost regional cloud adoption rates on April 2, 2024 (Ray Sharma, 2024-04-02).

  • Awards and Recognition: CoreStack won the Top Cloud Security Automation Award at the RSA Conference 2024 on May 6, 2024 (Business Wire, 2024-05-06). The company was also ranked as the 219th fastest-growing company in North America on the 2023 Deloitte Technology Fast 500™ (Yahoo Finance, 2023-11-08).

  • Product Launches: CoreStack unveiled a new cloud assessment product for Managed Service Providers (MSPs) on September 27, 2023 (Morningstar, 2023-09-27).

Market Position

CoreStack operates in the rapidly growing cloud management market, projected to reach an addressable market of $32 billion by 2021. The company competes with various cloud management platforms, including CloudBolt, CloudCheckr, and DoiT. CoreStack's unique value proposition lies in its AI-driven governance capabilities and its ability to provide a comprehensive view of cloud resources across multiple providers (Puskoor, 2020-03-30).

In conclusion, CoreStack is a promising player in the cloud management space, with a strong product offering and a seasoned leadership team. Its focus on AI-driven governance and multi-cloud management positions it well for future growth.
