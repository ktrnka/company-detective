About CoreStack

CoreStack, founded on November 4, 2016, is a company specializing in AI-powered multicloud governance solutions. Headquartered in Bellevue, Washington, CoreStack provides a platform that enables enterprises to achieve continuous and autonomous cloud governance, thereby accelerating innovation and delivering predictable outcomes (Crunchbase, 2025).

CoreStack's primary product is its multicloud governance platform, which offers comprehensive visibility and control over cloud resources. The platform is designed to help organizations reduce cloud costs by up to 40% and increase operational efficiency by 50% (CoreStack, 2024). CoreStack serves a diverse clientele across various industries, including finance, healthcare, education, telecommunications, technology, and government, and is recognized for its compliance with standards such as ISO, FedRAMP, and NIST (CoreStack, 2024).

The company generates revenue through its cloud governance solutions, which are sold to enterprises in highly regulated industries that require stringent cloud governance and compliance measures. CoreStack's platform is used by many leading global enterprises across multiple industries (Crunchbase, 2025).

CoreStack has demonstrated significant growth, ranking 235 on the 2024 Deloitte Technology Fast 500™ with a revenue growth of 505% from 2020 to 2023 (Khatri, Deloitte, 2024). The company has raised a total of $45 million in funding, with the most recent round of $30 million occurring on November 24, 2021 (Crunchbase, 2025).

Key Personnel

CoreStack's leadership team comprises experienced professionals with deep expertise in cloud technologies and governance. The CEO, Ez Natarajan, has been noted for his leadership in driving the company's innovation and growth in the cloud governance space (Khatri, Deloitte, 2024).
