About CoreStack

CoreStack, founded on November 4, 2016, is a global leader in multi-cloud governance solutions, headquartered in Bellevue, Washington. The company has expanded its operations with additional offices in Dubai, Chennai, and Hyderabad. CoreStack specializes in providing AI-powered solutions that help organizations manage their cloud resources effectively, ensuring compliance and automating cloud operations. The company primarily operates in the B2B sector, serving over 750 global enterprises across various industries, including highly regulated sectors, managing more than $2 billion in annual cloud consumption (CoreStack, Company Profile).

CoreStack's flagship product is the CR360™ platform, which offers a comprehensive suite of governance modules for FinOps, SecOps, and CloudOps. This platform provides a 360-degree view of cloud resources, enabling organizations to control costs, enhance security, improve compliance, and streamline operations across multiple cloud providers such as AWS, Azure, GCP, and OCI (CoreStack, The CoreStack Platform, 2024-07-04).

The company has demonstrated significant growth, with a 505% revenue increase from 2020 to 2023, and has been recognized on the Deloitte Technology Fast 500™ list for three consecutive years (CoreStack, Deloitte Technology Fast 500, 2024-11-22). CoreStack's revenue and customer base have expanded rapidly, supported by strategic partnerships and acquisitions, such as the acquisition of Karthik Consulting to enhance its federal market presence (Business Wire, 2024-12-10).

CoreStack's products are distributed through direct sales and partnerships, including collaborations with companies like ConRes and ZainTech to boost regional cloud adoption rates (Business Wire, 2024-10-21), (Ray Sharma, 2024-04-02).

Key Personnel

Ez Natarajan serves as the CEO of CoreStack. Under his leadership, the company has achieved significant growth and recognition in the cloud governance space. Natarajan has been instrumental in steering CoreStack towards its current trajectory, emphasizing innovation and a strong workplace culture (CoreStack, Inc. Best Workplaces, 2024-06-18).

Market Position

CoreStack operates in the rapidly growing multi-cloud management market, which is projected to expand significantly. The cloud automation segment alone accounted for a revenue share of 25.38% in 2022, indicating strong demand for effective cloud management solutions (Grand View Research, 2022-07-31). CoreStack's focus on AI-driven governance positions it well to capitalize on this growth.

CoreStack is a dynamic and rapidly growing company in the cloud governance space, known for its innovative solutions and strong workplace culture.
