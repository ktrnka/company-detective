About CoreStack

CoreStack, founded on November 4, 2016, is a company specializing in AI-powered multi-cloud governance solutions. Headquartered in Bellevue, Washington, with development operations in Chennai, India, CoreStack provides a platform that enables enterprises to achieve continuous and autonomous cloud governance at scale. The company is recognized for its innovative approach to cloud governance, having been featured in Gartner's Magic Quadrant for Cloud Management Platforms and as an IDC Innovator in Multi-cloud Management Platforms in 2020 (Khatri, CoreStack, 2020).

CoreStack's flagship product, CR360™, offers a comprehensive view of cloud resources across multiple platforms, including AWS, Azure, GCP, and OCI. The platform integrates capabilities such as FinOps, SecOps, and CloudOps, allowing organizations to manage their cloud resources efficiently (CoreStack, 2024). CoreStack serves a diverse clientele, including major enterprises across the U.S., Europe, and India, focusing on cloud governance and compliance needs (Khatri, CoreStack, 2020).

The company has raised a total of $45.7 million across four funding rounds, with its latest Series B round in November 2021, securing $30 million led by Avatar Growth Capital (Tracxn, 2021). CoreStack has reported annual growth rates exceeding 200% over the past three years and has been recognized on the Inc. 5000 list for fastest-growing companies (Business Wire, 2023).

CoreStack's products are distributed through strategic partnerships and collaborations with companies like ConRes IT Solutions and ZainTech, enhancing cloud management, cost optimization, and security capabilities for organizations in regulated industries (Khatri, CoreStack, 2024).
