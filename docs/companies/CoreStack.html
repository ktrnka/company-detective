About CoreStack

CoreStack, founded on November 4, 2016, is a company specializing in multi-cloud governance, offering an AI-powered platform designed to enhance cloud management and governance. Headquartered in Bellevue, Washington, CoreStack provides a suite of cloud governance solutions, including CoreStack Governance, CoreStack Assessments, and CR360™. These products help organizations manage costs, enhance security, ensure compliance, and streamline operations across multiple cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, GCP, and OCI (Crunchbase, 2025).

CoreStack operates primarily in the B2B sector, serving a diverse range of clients, including those in highly regulated industries. Notable customers include major tech companies like Synopsys, which have utilized CoreStack's solutions to achieve compliance across thousands of cloud resources (CoreStack, 2024-07-04). The company generates revenue through its cloud governance platform, which is distributed as a SaaS solution, allowing enterprises to manage their cloud resources effectively.

CoreStack has experienced significant growth, being recognized in the Deloitte Technology Fast 500™ and the Inc. 5000 list of America’s fastest-growing private companies. The company has raised a total of $45 million in funding, with the most recent round being $30 million on November 24, 2021 (Crunchbase, 2025). CoreStack's platform is used by many global enterprises across multiple industries, reflecting its scale and impact in the cloud governance space.

Third parties describe CoreStack as a leader in cloud governance, with recognition from Gartner's Magic Quadrant for Cloud Management Platforms and various industry awards, such as the Gold Stevie® Award for Cloud Governance Excellence (CoreStack, 2022-08-04).

Key Personnel

CoreStack's leadership team includes industry veterans with extensive experience in enterprise software and IT infrastructure. Many of the executives have backgrounds at Mindtree and have contributed to the development of Microsoft Azure (Puskoor, 2020-03-30). The company has recently strengthened its leadership with the addition of a Chief Revenue Officer and a Chief Marketing Officer, both bringing valuable experience from previous growth companies (Puskoor, 2020-03-30).
