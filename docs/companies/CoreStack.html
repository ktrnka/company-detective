About CoreStack

CoreStack, founded in 2016, is a global provider of AI-powered multi-cloud governance solutions. Headquartered in Bellevue, Washington, the company focuses on enabling enterprises to manage their cloud resources effectively while ensuring compliance, security, and cost efficiency. CoreStack's primary product is the NextGen Cloud Governance platform, which includes modules for FinOps, SecOps, and CloudOps. This platform is designed to integrate seamlessly with major cloud providers such as AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform, and Oracle Cloud (CoreStack Blog, 2023).

The company operates on a B2B model, serving over 750 global enterprises and managing more than $2 billion in annual cloud consumption (ZainTECH, 2024). CoreStack's revenue model is likely based on subscription fees for its cloud governance platform, although specific revenue figures are not publicly disclosed. The company has raised a total of $45.7 million in funding, with the latest Series B round of $30 million in November 2021 led by Avatar Growth Capital (Tracxn, 2021).

CoreStack has been recognized for its rapid growth, making the Inc. 5000 list of America's fastest-growing private companies for two consecutive years, ranking 12th in the Seattle area in 2024 (CoreStack Blog, 2024). The company employs approximately 240 people (ZainTECH, 2024).

Key Personnel

  • Ezhilarasan Natarajan: CEO of CoreStack. Natarajan emphasizes the importance of intelligent cloud governance and has been a vocal advocate for moving beyond reactive cloud management to a more strategic, automated approach (CoreStack, 2023).

  • Robert Ford: Chief Strategy Officer. Ford has authored white papers on cloud governance, highlighting the need for strategic approaches to maximize cloud investments (CoreStack, 2023).
