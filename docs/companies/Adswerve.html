About Adswerve

Adswerve Inc. is a prominent data, media, and technology consultancy that specializes in optimizing digital marketing efforts for brands and agencies. Founded in Denver, Colorado, Adswerve has established itself as a leader in the digital marketing space, leveraging advanced technologies and data analytics to deliver tailored solutions that enhance customer experiences and drive return on investment (ROI). The company operates primarily in a B2B model, serving a diverse range of clients across various industries.

Adswerve's services include technology solutions such as data aggregation, automation, and modeling, digital media management, attribution and reporting, and personalization and optimization. The company partners with leading platforms like Google and Adobe, providing specialized services in analytics and marketing technology. Adswerve is recognized as a Google Marketing Partner and an Adobe Solution Partner of the Year, underscoring its expertise and influence in the industry (Adswerve, n.d.).

The company employs approximately 300 people and promotes a virtual-first workplace culture, allowing employees to work from various locations, including the U.S., Canada, South Africa, and Europe. Adswerve emphasizes diversity, equity, and inclusion within its workforce (Adswerve, 2024-03-26).

Adswerve's revenue and financial performance details are not explicitly mentioned in the available sources, but the company's recognition and recent acquisitions suggest a strong financial position and growth trajectory. The company has demonstrated its ability to drive cost efficiencies for clients, such as saving The Arena Group over $250,000 in the first year through optimized Google Cloud solutions (Adswerve, 2024-01-30).

Key Personnel

Roger Berdusco is the CEO of Adswerve, playing a crucial role in steering the company's growth and strategic direction. He has emphasized the importance of innovation and collaboration in achieving customer success, particularly in the context of the company's recognition as the 2024 Adobe Digital Experience Emerging Partner of the Year – Americas (Adswerve, 2024-03-25).

News

Recent Acquisitions

In January 2024, Adswerve acquired MiaProva, a marketing technology firm specializing in Adobe Target and Adobe Analytics. This acquisition aims to enhance Adswerve's digital marketing capabilities and solidify its position as a premier digital consultancy. Brian Hawkins, the founder of MiaProva, brings nearly two decades of experience in marketing technology to Adswerve (Adswerve, 2024-01-30).

Partnerships and Collaborations

Adswerve has established strong partnerships with major platforms, including Google and Adobe, to provide clients with advanced marketing solutions. The company has been recognized for its ability to drive customer success through these collaborations, particularly in leveraging Adobe's suite of tools for analytics and customer journey optimization (Adswerve, 2024-03-25).

Product Developments

Adswerve offers a range of services and products designed to optimize digital marketing efforts. Key offerings include the Google Marketing Platform, Adobe Customer Journey Analytics, and Privacy Sandbox Solutions. These products focus on integrating advanced analytics, data management, and marketing technology to help clients achieve their business goals (Adswerve, 2024-01-30).
