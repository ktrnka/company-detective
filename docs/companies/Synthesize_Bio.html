About Synthesize Bio

Synthesize Bio is a biotechnology company that focuses on developing innovative solutions for biomanufacturing and environmental sustainability. The company leverages advanced biotechnological processes to create bio-based materials and products, contributing to a circular economy and addressing global challenges such as pollution and resource depletion.
