About Synthesize Bio

Synthesize Bio is a company dedicated to the development of bio-based materials, particularly focusing on sustainable solutions within the polymer industry. The company specializes in synthesizing bio-based polyurethanes (PUs) and other elastomers derived from renewable resources. This focus aligns with the increasing demand for environmentally friendly materials across various sectors, including packaging, automotive, and construction.
