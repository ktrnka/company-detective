About Synthesize Bio

Synthesize Bio is a biotechnology company focused on developing sustainable and bio-based materials, particularly in the field of elastomers and polymers. The company aims to address environmental concerns associated with traditional petroleum-based plastics by creating innovative solutions that utilize renewable resources. Synthesize Bio is involved in various research and development projects that explore the synthesis, properties, and applications of bio-based elastomers, contributing to a circular economy and sustainability in materials science.
