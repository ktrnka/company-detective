About Built In

Built In is a technology recruitment platform founded in 2011. It connects tech professionals with companies seeking to hire, serving as a significant player in the tech job market. The platform caters to millions of users monthly, providing resources for tech professionals to stay updated on industry trends, develop skills, and find job opportunities that align with their values. Built In serves over 2,000 customers, ranging from startups to Fortune 500 companies, helping them attract hard-to-reach tech talent (Built In, PR Newswire, 2024-01-09).

The company operates primarily in the B2B space, offering its services to businesses looking to recruit tech talent. Built In generates revenue through its recruitment services, which include job listings and company profiles that showcase company culture, values, and benefits. The platform's extensive reach and user engagement make it a valuable resource for both job seekers and employers in the tech industry (Built In, PR Newswire, 2024-01-09).

Key Personnel

Maria Christopoulos Katris is the Founder and CEO of Built In. She has emphasized the company's commitment to connecting tech professionals with employers that offer meaningful rewards and a supportive culture. Her leadership is focused on fostering a community where tech professionals can grow their careers by finding roles that offer the best rewards (Built In, PR Newswire, 2024-01-09).

In January 2024, Built In announced the winners of its sixth annual Best Places to Work awards program.
