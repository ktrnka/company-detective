About Countsy

Countsy is a back office outsourcing company that provides specialized services in accounting and HR/People Operations, primarily targeting startups, scaleups, and spinouts. Founded on January 1, 2017, and headquartered in San Francisco, California, Countsy offers a comprehensive suite of services, including access to a Fractional CFO and CPO, alongside a technology platform for managing billing, payroll, expense management, and reporting (Crunchbase, 2025).

Countsy operates under Resources Connection, Inc. (RGP), a global professional services firm with over 2,600 employees and a client base that includes 88% of the Fortune 100 companies. RGP reported revenues of $633 million in 2024, although it faced an 18% decline in sales due to economic challenges (RGP, Business Wire, 2025). Countsy's services are designed to alleviate the administrative burden on companies, allowing them to focus on core business activities (RGP, Business Wire, 2025).

Countsy's target customers are primarily venture-backed startups, scaleups, and spinouts. The company collaborates with venture capitalists and accelerators to support their portfolio companies in achieving operational readiness and scalability. Countsy's platform, launched in April 2024, integrates essential business tools and collaboration features, enhancing back-office management (InScope, PYMNTS, 2024).

Key Personnel

Kate Duchene is the CEO of RGP, the parent company of Countsy. She has been leading RGP since 2016 and has played a crucial role in navigating the company through economic challenges and restructuring its operations to better serve clients (RGP, OCBJ, 2024).

News

Product Developments

In April 2024, Countsy launched an online platform designed to simplify back-office management. This platform includes features for enterprise resource planning, payroll, accounts receivable and payable, financial reporting, and expense management (InScope, PYMNTS, 2024).

Strategic Changes

Countsy is part of RGP's broader strategy to provide comprehensive solutions to its clients. RGP has been actively restructuring and redesigning its operational model to better showcase its different business segments, including Countsy (RGP, OCBJ, 2024). Additionally, RGP relocated its headquarters from California to Texas to enhance operational efficiency and client support (RGP, OCBJ, 2024).

Client Feedback

Countsy has received positive feedback from clients for its ability to streamline operations and reduce complexity in financial processes. A notable example is a startup that grew from 35 to 250 employees in under two years while managing accounts payable with minimal staffing, thanks to Countsy's services (Consulting Partners, Tipalti, 2024).

Conclusion

Countsy, as part of RGP, is well-positioned in the outsourced services market, particularly for startups and growing companies. With a strong operational foundation, positive client feedback, and recent product innovations, Countsy is equipped to support businesses in navigating their growth challenges. The company's strategic direction and adaptability to market demands are key factors for prospective clients and investors to consider.
