About Actual AI

Actual AI, operating under the parent company SprintReview, Inc., was founded on June 21, 2024 (Crunchbase, 2025). The company focuses on enhancing software development processes by providing tools that bridge the gap between traditional performance metrics and actual developer productivity.

Products and Services

Actual AI offers a software service that automates the generation of commit messages, pull request summaries, and release notes. This is achieved by analyzing software development code and content from third-party platforms like Jira and GitHub. The service is designed to improve transparency and efficiency in software development. The company's main products include:

  • Automation Tool: This tool drafts commit messages, pull request summaries, and release notes.
  • SaaS Platform: Offers both hosted features and on-premises solutions tailored for software development teams.

Business Model and Customers

Actual AI operates on a B2B model, targeting software development teams and organizations that aim to enhance their engineering processes and improve developer satisfaction. The company employs a simple and transparent pricing model based on the GitHub seats model, which automatically adjusts charges based on usage.

Company Culture and Careers

Actual AI promotes a culture of transparency, collaboration, and user feedback. The company values justice in the workplace, ensuring that developers are recognized for their contributions and that performance reviews are fair and unbiased. The company is actively hiring for various positions, including:

  • Infrastructure Engineer: Responsible for backend architecture and infrastructure stability.
  • Frontend Engineer: Focuses on user experience and frontend development.
