About Rad AI

Rad AI, founded on January 1, 2018, by Dr. Jeff Chang and Doktor Gurson, is a company specializing in artificial intelligence solutions for the radiology sector. The company was established to address radiologist burnout and the lack of assistive technology in the field. Dr. Chang, the youngest radiologist in U.S. history, leveraged his background in machine learning and experience as an ER radiologist to develop AI solutions tailored for radiology (Crunchbase, 2024).

Rad AI offers several products designed to enhance radiology workflows and improve patient care:

  • Rad AI Impressions: Automates the generation of radiology report impressions, reducing the cognitive load on radiologists and allowing them to dictate up to 35% fewer words.
  • Rad AI Continuity: A follow-up management platform that tracks incidental findings and ensures timely follow-ups.
  • Rad AI Reporting: A tool for creating and managing medical imaging reports with structured reporting capabilities and advanced speech recognition.

The company serves over 100 healthcare organizations, including some of the largest health systems in the U.S., and is utilized by 30% of U.S. radiology practices, including 8 of the 10 largest private radiology practices (Rad AI, 2024-01-16). Rad AI operates primarily in a B2B model, targeting radiologists, healthcare executives, and IT teams.

Rad AI has raised a total of $83.25 million in funding, with a recent Series B round of $50 million led by Khosla Ventures (TechCrunch, 2024-05-07). The company has been recognized as one of the most promising healthcare AI companies, appearing on CB Insights’ 2023 Digital Health 50 and winning AuntMinnie’s “Best New Radiology Software” award in 2023 (Rad AI, 2024-01-16).
