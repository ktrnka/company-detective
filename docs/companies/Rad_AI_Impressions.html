About Rad AI

Rad AI, founded on January 1, 2018, by Dr. Jeff Chang and Doktor Gurson, is a company specializing in artificial intelligence solutions for the radiology sector. The company aims to address radiologist burnout and improve patient care by automating repetitive tasks in radiology workflows. Rad AI's products are utilized by approximately 30% of U.S. radiology practices and health systems, serving over 50 million patients annually (Rad AI, PR Newswire, 2024-01-16).

Rad AI generates revenue through its B2B model, partnering with healthcare organizations such as Cone Health, Inland Imaging, and Radiology Associates of North Texas (News). The company has raised a total of $83.25 million in funding, with a recent $50 million Series B round led by Khosla Ventures (TechCrunch, 2024-05-07).

The company's product offerings include:

  • Rad AI Impressions: An AI-driven tool that auto-generates the impression section of radiology reports.
  • Rad AI Continuity: A follow-up management platform for tracking incidental findings.
  • Rad AI Reporting: A digital tool for creating and managing radiology reports.
  • Rad AI Nexus: A worklist solution that prioritizes tasks for radiologists.

Rad AI's products are distributed through partnerships with healthcare organizations, and the company has recently expanded internationally by partnering with MIC Medical Imaging in Alberta, Canada (Rad AI Finalizes First Partnership Outside the US With MIC Medical Imaging of Alberta, Canada, 2024-07-26).

Key Personnel

  • Dr. Jeff Chang: Co-founder and Chief Product Officer, known for his insights into workflow automation in healthcare. He emphasizes the importance of human oversight in AI applications (Dr. Sai Balasubramanian, Forbes, 2024-01-16).
  • Doktor Gurson: Co-founder and CEO, with a background in technology and entrepreneurship. He is committed to transforming the radiology reporting landscape (Rad AI, PR Newswire, 2024-01-16).
  • Reed Humphrey: VP of Strategic Partnerships, focusing on health system sales.
  • Josh Duncan: Senior VP of Sales, Marketing, and Customer Success, instrumental in expanding Rad AI's partnership base.
