About Rad AI

Rad AI is a rapidly expanding company founded in 2018 by Dr. Jeff Chang and Doktor Gurson. The company specializes in artificial intelligence (AI) solutions aimed at enhancing radiology workflows, reducing radiologist burnout, and improving patient care. Rad AI's products, such as Rad AI Omni and Rad AI Continuity, automate various aspects of radiology reporting and follow-up management. The company operates primarily in the B2B sector, serving approximately 30% of U.S. radiology practices and health systems, which collectively cater to over 50 million patients annually (Rad AI, PR Newswire, 2024).

Rad AI generates revenue through the sale and integration of its AI solutions into healthcare systems. The company has raised over $80 million in funding, including a $50 million Series B round led by Khosla Ventures (TechCrunch, 2024). Rad AI's products are distributed through partnerships with healthcare providers and collaborations with major companies like Google Cloud and Bayer, which help integrate Rad AI's solutions into broader healthcare platforms (Rad AI, PR Newswire, 2024).

Key Personnel

  • Dr. Jeff Chang: Co-founder of Rad AI, known for being the youngest radiologist in U.S. history. His expertise in radiology and AI drives the company's mission to improve radiology workflows.
  • Doktor Gurson: Co-founder and CEO of Rad AI. Gurson emphasizes the company's mission to reduce radiologist burnout and improve patient care through innovative AI solutions (Rad AI, PR Newswire, 2024).

Partnerships and Collaborations

Google Cloud Partnership

In January 2024, Rad AI announced a collaboration with Google Cloud to enhance its platforms, including Rad AI Omni Impressions and Rad AI Reporting, by integrating advanced generative AI models. This partnership aims to improve report quality and consistency (Rad AI, PR Newswire, 2024).

Bayer Collaboration

In June 2024, Rad AI partnered with Bayer to integrate its AI solutions into Bayer's Calantic Digital Solutions platform. This collaboration provides hospitals and health systems with access to Rad AI's generative AI capabilities, enhancing operational efficiency in radiology (Bayer, Business Wire, 2024).

LucidHealth Partnership

Rad AI finalized a partnership with LucidHealth, allowing its radiologists to access Rad AI Omni Impressions. This partnership is expected to improve reporting efficiency and reduce burnout among radiologists (LucidHealth, 2024).

Product Innovations

Rad AI recently launched Omni Unchanged, a feature that allows radiologists to dictate complex follow-up exams up to 50% faster by automatically extracting stable findings from prior reports. This innovation exemplifies Rad AI's commitment to reducing cognitive load and enhancing reporting efficiency (Rad AI, PR Newswire, 2023).
