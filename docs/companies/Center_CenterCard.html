About Center

Center, officially known as Center ID Corp., is a financial technology company founded on January 1, 2018. The company is headquartered in Washington State, USA, and specializes in providing real-time expense management solutions. Center's primary focus is on automating corporate spend management, offering tools that help businesses efficiently track and manage their expenses. The company operates primarily through its flagship product, the CenterCard, a corporate credit card issued by Comdata, Inc. under a license from Mastercard Inc. (Crunchbase, 2025).

Center's business model is B2B, targeting small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) with its integrated corporate card and expense solution. This solution is designed to save time, improve operations and compliance, and deliver real-time insights to finance teams for better decision-making. The company generates revenue through its financial technology services, although specific revenue figures are not disclosed in the available sources.

Center has received positive feedback from various clients, such as AMAROK, Statewide Materials, and the Museum of Glass, who have noted significant improvements in their financial processes due to Center's solutions (Center, 2024-02-06).
