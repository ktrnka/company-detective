About Center

Center is a software company headquartered in Bellevue, Washington, specializing in modernizing corporate card, travel, and employee expense management through its integrated solution. Founded in 2017, Center has quickly established itself in the market, raising over $140 million in funding, including a recent $30 million Series C round led by Top Tier Capital Partners in December 2023 (PR Newswire, 2023-12-05).

The company offers a comprehensive solution for real-time expense management through its CenterCard® Corporate Credit Card and integrated software. This platform is designed to streamline the expense reporting process, enhance visibility into employee spending, and simplify corporate travel management. Center's solutions are applicable across various industries, including manufacturing, consulting, and non-profits, particularly benefiting organizations with significant employee travel and complex expense reporting needs.

Center operates on a B2B model, targeting mid-market companies looking to streamline their expense management processes. The company generates revenue through a usage-based pricing model, eliminating upfront costs and allowing businesses to pay only for what they use (Center, 2024-01-30).

The company's products are distributed through strategic partnerships and direct sales, with notable collaborations including Direct Travel and Spotnana, enhancing its offerings in travel management (PR Newswire, 2024-04-03).

Key Personnel

  • Naveen Singh: CEO of Center. Singh has emphasized the importance of integrating payment platforms with existing software to enhance customer experience and reduce costs. He views a fully integrated approach as the next frontier of innovation for expense management (PR Newswire, 2023-12-05).

  • Amy Padgett: Appointed as Senior Vice President of Travel Ecosystem in May 2024. Padgett, a former SAP Concur executive, was brought on board to expand Center's travel management capabilities (Newsroom, 2024-05-21).

News

Funding and Growth

  • Series C Funding: In December 2023, Center raised $30 million in Series C funding to accelerate innovation and broaden distribution, following a $15 million Series B round in October 2022. This brings the total capital raised to over $140 million (PR Newswire, 2023-12-05).

  • Customer Growth: The company reported a 64% year-over-year increase in customer growth, indicating strong market demand for its solutions (PR Newswire, 2023-12-05).

Partnerships

  • Direct Travel Integration: In April 2024, Center was selected as a key component of Direct Travel's technology platform, aiming to redefine corporate travel management (PR Newswire, 2024-04-03).

  • Spotnana Collaboration: Center has integrated Spotnana's Travel-as-a-Service platform into its expense management solution, providing a comprehensive travel and expense management experience (PR Newswire, 2023-10-17).
