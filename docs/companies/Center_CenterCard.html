About Center

Center is a software company founded on January 1, 2018, and is based in Bellevue, Washington. The company focuses on modernizing corporate card, travel, and employee expense management through a unified solution. Center's primary product is the CenterCard, a corporate credit card issued by Comdata, Inc., under a license from Mastercard. This card is integrated with Center's software platform, which provides real-time visibility into employee spending and automates the expensing process (Crunchbase, 2025).

Center operates on a usage-based business model, requiring no upfront investment from its customers. The company serves businesses across various industries, primarily targeting small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). Center's solutions are designed to save time, improve operations and compliance, and deliver real-time insights to finance teams for better decision-making (Center, PR Newswire, 2024-04-03).

The company has raised a total of $101 million in funding, with the most recent round of $30 million occurring on December 5, 2023. This financial backing supports Center's growth and expansion efforts (Crunchbase, 2025).

Key Personnel

Naveen Singh serves as the CEO of Center. He has been vocal about the company's mission to address long-standing issues in corporate travel management, emphasizing the importance of integrating travel booking and expensing processes to enhance the user experience (Center, PR Newswire, 2024-04-03).

Recent Developments

Partnership with Direct Travel

On April 3, 2024, Center announced a partnership with Direct Travel, a leader in corporate travel management. This collaboration aims to create a modern integrated travel technology stack, enhancing the corporate travel experience for Direct Travel's clients. Steve Singh, Executive Chairman of Direct Travel, highlighted the benefits of this partnership, stating that "Center empowers our customers to holistically manage their travel and expense programs" (Center, PR Newswire, 2024-04-03).

Strategic Acquisitions

Direct Travel was recently acquired by an investment group led by Steve Singh, indicating strong backing and potential for growth in the corporate travel sector. This acquisition aligns with Center's mission to redefine corporate travel and expense management (Center, PR Newswire, 2024-04-03).

Expansion of Services

Center is expanding its capabilities by integrating AI-driven innovations with its technology stack. This expansion aims to deliver a better value proposition to business travelers and their companies, further solidifying Center's position in the market (Center, PR Newswire, 2024-04-03).

Product Overview: CenterCard

CenterCard is a corporate credit card designed for business use, providing companies with a streamlined approach to managing travel and expenses. The card is integrated with Center's software, allowing for real-time visibility into spending and automated expense reporting.

Key Features

  • Unified Travel and Expense Management: CenterCard integrates travel booking and expense management into one system, providing a seamless experience for users. This includes features such as real-time expense tracking, intuitive booking processes, and comprehensive travel inventory (Center, PR Newswire, 2024-04-03).

  • User Experience: The platform offers a consumer-grade experience with a user-friendly interface for booking trips, managing itineraries, and submitting expenses. It also includes loyalty program integration and flexible management options for itinerary changes (Center, Travelers by Director of Accounting, 2024-10-09).

  • Support and Compliance: Center provides 24/7 assistance and proactive notifications to ensure a smooth travel experience. The platform emphasizes compliance with company policies, offering clear policy guidance and automated compliance checks (Center, Travelers by Director of Accounting, 2024-10-09).

Financial Aspects

Center provides its spend management services at no charge, but this may change in the future. Users should consult their Cardholder Agreement for any associated fees, terms, and conditions (Center, 2024-02-06).

Conclusion

Center is strategically positioned to redefine corporate travel and expense management through its innovative CenterCard and integrated software solutions. The recent partnership with Direct Travel and the focus on enhancing user experience and compliance are significant steps toward achieving its goals. With substantial funding and a clear vision, Center is poised for growth in the evolving landscape of corporate travel management.
