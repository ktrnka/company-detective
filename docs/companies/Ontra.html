About Ontra

Ontra, founded in 2014 and headquartered in San Francisco, is a prominent provider of AI-powered legal technology solutions tailored for private capital investment firms. The company has developed a comprehensive Legal Operating System that integrates advanced software with a global network of over 600 legal professionals. This system is designed to automate and streamline legal workflows across the entire fund lifecycle (News Room, 2021-07-07).

As of December 2022, Ontra employed approximately 179 individuals and has raised a total of $240 million in funding, with its latest round being a Series B of $200 million led by Blackstone in October 2021 (Tracxn, 2021-10-28). Ontra's revenue details are not explicitly mentioned, but the scale of its operations and partnerships with over 700 private capital investment firms, including nine of the top ten private equity firms, suggests a significant market presence (Ontra, 2024-02-06).

Ontra's flagship product, the Contract Automation platform, facilitates the negotiation and management of contracts within the private capital markets. The platform has processed over one million contracts, serving more than a thousand global private markets firms. The company's products are distributed through direct partnerships with investment banks, private equity firms, and AM Law 100 law firms (Ontra, 2024-05-15).

Key Personnel

  • Troy Pospisil: CEO and Founder of Ontra. He has emphasized the importance of AI in transforming legal workflows and is committed to integrating additional AI capabilities to accelerate routine contract negotiations (Ontra, 2024-02-06).
  • Leslie Olsen: Chief Marketing Officer, appointed to enhance customer relationships and expand market reach (Ontra, 2024-03-06).
  • John Lofy: Senior Vice President of Customer Success, focusing on improving customer experiences and ensuring high-quality service (Ontra, 2024-04-02).
  • Frank Giovinazzo: General Manager of Contract Automation, noted for his role in integrating GPT-4 to enhance the platform's capabilities (Ontra, 2024-02-06).

AI Integration

Ontra has integrated GPT-4 into its Contract Automation platform, significantly accelerating contract negotiations and expanding the types of documents it can handle. This integration is part of Ontra's ongoing commitment to leverage AI to improve efficiency and reduce friction in the deal-making process (Ontra, 2024-02-06).

Executive Appointments

The company has made several key executive appointments, including Leslie Olsen as Chief Marketing Officer and John Lofy as Senior Vice President of Customer Success. These appointments reflect Ontra's focus on enhancing customer relationships and expanding its market reach (Ontra, 2024-03-06); (Ontra, 2024-04-02).

Product Expansion

Ontra has launched new products such as Ontra Atlas, a specialized entity management solution, and has plans to further develop its offerings to meet the evolving needs of private capital markets (Ontra, 2024-03-06); (Ontra, 2024-05-15).
