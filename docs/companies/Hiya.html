About Hiya

Hiya is a company that specializes in enhancing voice communication through caller identification and spam-blocking solutions. Founded on April 27, 2016, Hiya has developed a range of products aimed at improving the phone experience for users worldwide. The company is known for its mobile caller ID applications and network-based spam-blocking solutions, serving over 500 million users globally (Crunchbase, 2025).

Hiya's product offerings include:

  • Hiya Protect: A network-based solution that blocks spam and fraud calls.
  • Hiya Connect: A branded call platform that allows businesses to display their identity on outbound calls, improving answer rates and customer trust.
  • Call Inspection: A service that audits how business calls appear on customer phones, helping to identify spam labeling issues.
  • APIs for Developers: Tools for detecting AI-generated voices, preventing voice deepfake attacks, and implementing call blocking.

Hiya operates primarily in the B2B space, partnering with major carriers and technology companies such as AT&T, T-Mobile, and Samsung to integrate its services into their offerings. The company also provides a consumer app available on Google Android and iPhone, which is integrated into the phone experience for users of AT&T Call Protect, T-Mobile Name ID, and Samsung Galaxy devices (Crunchbase, 2025).

Hiya's revenue and financial performance details are not explicitly mentioned in the available sources, but the company has raised a total of $22 million in funding, with the most recent round of $4 million occurring on March 27, 2023 (Crunchbase, 2025).

Key Personnel

  • Alex Algard: CEO of Hiya. Algard has been instrumental in driving the company's vision and growth in the voice intelligence space. His leadership has been pivotal in Hiya's development of innovative solutions to enhance voice communication (Hiya News, 2025-01-28).

News

Product Developments

  • In January 2025, Hiya launched the Hiya AI Phone, a mobile app designed to enhance call management for busy professionals. This app features intelligent call screening, scam protection, and voice deepfake detection, addressing growing concerns around phone scams and unwanted calls (Hiya News, 2025-01-28).

Partnerships and Collaborations

  • Hiya has established partnerships with major carriers and technology companies, including Samsung and Ericsson, to enhance its call protection services. The Hiya Network currently serves over 450 million users globally, providing call protection and identity services (Hiya News, 2024-05-07).

Market Position and Performance

  • Hiya has been recognized as a leader in voice security and identity, ranking as a High Performer in G2's Spring 2024 Grid Report for VoIP Providers. The company received high marks for ease of setup and customer satisfaction, with an average rating of 4.8 out of 5 stars from verified users (Hiya News, 2024-05-07).

Recent Developments

  • Hiya has launched several initiatives to combat the rise of deepfake voice scams and enhance call protection services. The company has received recognition for its solutions, earning multiple badges across various G2 reports for its branded caller ID solutions (Hiya News, 2024-08-22).

Industry Impact

  • Hiya's adaptive AI technology has been instrumental in blocking over 150 million scam calls through O2 UK's 'Call Defence' service, highlighting the significant demand for its services in combating phone fraud and spam calls (Leo Tushar, 2025-03-19).

Hiya continues to innovate in the voice communication space, leveraging AI to enhance its call protection capabilities and maintain its position as a leader in the industry.
