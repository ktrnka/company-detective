About DoubleDown Interactive

DoubleDown Interactive Co., Ltd. is a global gaming company that specializes in developing and publishing casual games for mobile and desktop platforms. Founded on January 1, 2010, the company is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea. It is a wholly owned subsidiary of International Game Technology (NYSE: IGT) (Crunchbase, 2024). The company is renowned for its flagship product, DoubleDown Casino, a leading social casino game that offers a variety of slots and table games, including Blackjack, Roulette, and Video Poker. DoubleDown Interactive has also expanded into the iGaming sector through the acquisition of SuprNation, a European iGaming operator (DoubleDown Interactive, GlobeNewswire, 2024-10-10).

DoubleDown Interactive operates primarily in the B2C market, targeting casual players who enjoy social casino games. The company generates revenue through in-app purchases and monetization strategies within its games. In the second quarter of 2024, DoubleDown reported a revenue of $88.2 million, a 17% increase from the previous year, with significant contributions from SuprNation (DoubleDown Interactive, GlobeNewswire, 2024-08-12).

The company's products are distributed digitally, available on both mobile and web platforms, which has helped it reach millions of players worldwide. Over time, DoubleDown Interactive has expanded its product offerings to include games like Undead World: Hero Survival and Cash Me Out Games, diversifying its portfolio beyond social casino games (DoubleDown Interactive, GlobeNewswire, 2024-10-10).

Third-party descriptions of DoubleDown Interactive often highlight its leadership in the social casino segment and its strategic growth through acquisitions. The company describes itself as committed to providing unrivaled online social casino experiences (Crunchbase, 2024).
