About vcita

vcita is a business management platform founded in 2010, designed to support small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs). The company operates under the brand inTandem and has offices in Bellevue, Washington, and Tel-Aviv, Israel. vcita provides a suite of tools aimed at automating and streamlining business operations for SMBs, including invoicing, billing, scheduling, client management, and more. The platform is particularly tailored for service providers, helping them enhance client interactions and operational efficiency (vcita, 2025-01-02).

As of 2023, vcita employs over 160 individuals and serves more than 100,000 business owners globally, including barbers, law firms, and fitness trainers. The company generates revenue by offering its software solutions to these businesses, operating primarily in a B2B model (vcita, 2025-01-02). While specific revenue figures are not publicly disclosed, vcita is positioned within a growing market for small business management solutions, with increasing demand for tools that streamline operations and improve customer engagement (PYMNTS, 2024-11-12).

vcita's products are distributed through its digital platform, allowing users to access and integrate various functionalities directly into their business operations. The company has also formed strategic partnerships, such as with Mastercard, to expand its reach and capabilities (Digital Commerce 360, 2024-11-13).

Key Personnel

Itzik Levy is the founder and CEO of vcita. Under his leadership, the company has focused on empowering small businesses through accessible technology and continuous improvement of its platform (Digital Commerce 360, 2024-11-13).
