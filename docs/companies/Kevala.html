About Kevala

Kevala is a healthcare staffing and workforce management platform founded in early 2020, based in Seattle. The company focuses on optimizing scheduling, improving staffing efficiency, and enhancing communication within healthcare facilities, particularly in senior housing and skilled nursing communities. Kevala's platform integrates advanced scheduling, credential management, vendor management, and data-driven insights to optimize staff utilization, improve care delivery, reduce costs, and enhance the overall resident experience (Integral Senior Living, 2024).

Kevala operates as a B2B company, primarily serving healthcare facilities such as senior housing and skilled nursing communities. The company generates revenue by providing its workforce management solutions to these facilities, helping them manage their staffing needs more efficiently. Kevala has partnered with organizations like Integral Senior Living to implement its platform across multiple facilities, demonstrating its scale and reach within the industry (Integral Senior Living, 2024).

As a venture-backed startup, Kevala has raised a total of $35.8 million across three funding rounds, with the most recent Series A round raising $14.8 million on May 21, 2024. This funding reflects strong investor confidence in Kevala's business model and growth potential (Tracxn, 2024).

Kevala's products are distributed through partnerships with healthcare facilities, where the platform is integrated into existing systems to streamline workforce management processes. The company has been described as a leader in healthcare staffing solutions, focusing on flexibility, efficiency, and improved communication to enhance the overall experience for healthcare providers and staff (Kevala, 2024).

Key Personnel

Lauren Wilson was appointed as Vice President and General Manager on August 1, 2024, to lead Kevala's Senior Housing and Long-Term Care business segment. Wilson brings extensive experience in healthcare and senior housing, and her appointment is seen as a strategic move to drive innovation and growth within the company (Kevala, 2024).

Partnerships

On January 17, 2024, Kevala expanded its partnership with Integral Senior Living, a prominent senior living community management company. This partnership involves implementing Kevala's scheduling platform across all 65 of Integral's assisted living facilities, aiming to enhance staffing optimization and provide real-time insights into operational health (Integral Senior Living, 2024).

Executive Appointments

Kevala appointed Lauren Wilson as Vice President and General Manager on August 1, 2024, to lead its Senior Housing and Long-Term Care business segment. Wilson's appointment is part of Kevala's strategy to institute significant changes in the industry by empowering staff and administrators with innovative tools (Kevala, 2024).
