About Clobotics

Clobotics is a technology company founded on November 1, 2016, by Claire Chen, George Yan, Yan Ke, and Zhao Li. The company is headquartered in Shanghai, China, with additional offices in Seattle, Washington. Clobotics specializes in providing intelligent computer vision solutions for the retail and wind power sectors. Their end-to-end solutions integrate computer vision, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data analytics software with various hardware, including autonomous drones and IoT devices, to automate operational processes and enhance efficiency (Crunchbase, 2024).

Products and Services

  • Wind Services: Clobotics offers comprehensive inspection, repair, and maintenance services for wind turbines globally. Their SmartView solution uses advanced computer vision and machine learning technologies to capture and analyze data from drone inspections, providing detailed insights into the condition of wind turbine blades (Coca-Cola Co Digital Transformation Strategy Report, 2024).

  • Retail Services: The company utilizes computer vision and IoT devices to deliver insights and decision-making support for sales and marketing strategies within retail environments (Clobotics Company Profile, 2024).

Customers and Market

Clobotics serves a diverse range of customers in the wind energy and retail industries, focusing on enhancing operational capabilities and outcomes. The company operates in a B2B model, providing solutions to businesses in these sectors. The aerial inspection service market, where Clobotics is a key player, is projected to grow significantly, driven by advancements in drone technology and increasing demand for efficient inspection solutions (Aerial Inspection Service Market Size, 2024).

Financials and Scale

Clobotics has raised a total of $83.9 million across six funding rounds, with notable investors including Tiger Global Management, GGV Capital, and CDIB Capital Group. The company is recognized as a "soonicorn," indicating its potential to achieve unicorn status soon (Tracxn, 2021). As of the latest reports, Clobotics employs between 51 to 100 individuals (Top 300 Startups in China, 2024).

Company Evolution

Since its founding, Clobotics has focused on leveraging technology to improve operational efficiency and safety in the industries it serves. The company has filed more than 30 patents and continues to innovate with a strong international research and development team (Crunchbase, 2024).

He emphasizes the importance of high-quality data in aerial inspections, highlighting the company's commitment to leveraging technology for improved operational outcomes (Inspecting Inside and Out for Blade Hazards, 2024).
