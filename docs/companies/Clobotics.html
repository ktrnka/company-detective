About Clobotics

Clobotics is a technology company founded on November 1, 2016, with headquarters in Shanghai, China, and Seattle, Washington. The company specializes in providing intelligent computer vision solutions for the retail and wind power sectors. Clobotics integrates computer vision, artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning, and data analytics with various hardware, including autonomous drones and IoT devices, to automate operational processes and enhance efficiency (Crunchbase, 2025).

The company operates primarily in two verticals:

  • Wind Services: Clobotics offers drone-based inspections of wind turbine blades using its Clobotics IBIS™ system, which can inspect all three blades in 18-25 minutes. It also provides internal inspections with the Clobotics Crawler™ robot and uses the Clobotics IRIS™ portal for data management (Clobotics, 2024).

  • Retail Services: The company uses computer vision and IoT devices to provide insights and analytics for retail environments, enhancing sales and marketing strategies (Clobotics, 2024).

Clobotics serves a diverse range of clients in the wind energy sector, having conducted inspections on nearly 40,000 wind turbines in 2023. In retail, it partners with businesses like Coca-Cola to enhance operational insights (Clobotics, 2024); (Coca-Cola Co Digital Transformation Strategy Report 2024, 2024-07-01).

The company has raised approximately $63 million across six funding rounds, with notable investors including GGV Capital and Wangsu Science & Technology (Top 300 Startups in China in 2024, 2024-02-14). Clobotics is positioned in the rapidly growing aerial inspection service market, projected to expand significantly over the next decade (Aerial Inspection Service Market Size, Share, Outlook, Demand, 2023-12-31).

Key Personnel

Clobotics was founded by Claire Chen, George Yan, Yan Ke, and Zhao Li. Claire Chen, who serves as the COO, has been instrumental in driving the company's vision of digitizing the physical world through AI and data analytics (Top 300 Startups in China in 2024, 2024-02-14); (Clobotics, 2024).

Funding and Financial Performance

Clobotics has raised a total of $63 million, with significant funding rounds including $22 million in 2019 and $11 million in 2018 (Crunchbase, 2025). This financial backing has been crucial for developing its technology and expanding its market reach (Top 300 Startups in China in 2024, 2024-02-14).

Market Position and Developments

Clobotics is a key player in the aerial inspection market, leveraging AI and computer vision technologies to provide innovative solutions for the retail and wind power sectors. The company continues to innovate, focusing on enhancing its AI capabilities and expanding its service offerings (Aerial Inspection Service Market Key Players and Competitive Insights, 2023-12-31).

Recent developments include the release of Clobotics IBIS™ 4.8, which improves inspection speed and quality, and strategic acquisitions like Danish Blade Service and Finetune to strengthen its position in the wind repair market (Clobotics, 2024).

Industry Impact

Clobotics' technology is reshaping the wind turbine inspection market by reducing the time and labor costs associated with traditional methods. The company's solutions capture high-resolution aerial imagery, processed to generate precise inspection reports, facilitating proactive maintenance (Aerial Inspection Service Market Size, Share, Outlook, Demand, 2023-12-31).

In the retail sector, Clobotics' SmartView solution is used by Coca-Cola to monitor cooler data, showcasing the company's ability to integrate its technology into large-scale operations (Coca-Cola Co Digital Transformation Strategy Report 2024, 2024-07-01).

Overall, Clobotics is well-positioned to capitalize on the increasing demand for efficient inspection solutions across various industries, driven by technological innovation and the need for increased operational efficiency (Aerial Inspection Service Market Key Players and Competitive Insights, 2023-12-31).
