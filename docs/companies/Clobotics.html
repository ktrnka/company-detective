About Clobotics

Clobotics is a technology company founded on November 1, 2016, by Claire Chen, George Yan, Yan Ke, and Zhao Li. The company is headquartered in Shanghai, China, with additional offices in Seattle, Washington. Clobotics specializes in intelligent computer vision solutions, primarily serving the wind power and retail industries. The company's offerings integrate computer vision, artificial intelligence, and data analytics with various hardware, such as autonomous drones and IoT devices, to automate operational processes and enhance efficiency (Crunchbase, 2024).

Clobotics operates in a B2B model, serving enterprise clients like Coca-Cola, Procter & Gamble, General Electric, and Vattenfall. The company generates revenue through its comprehensive suite of solutions for wind turbine inspection, repair, and maintenance, as well as retail insights services. Clobotics' wind services include the Clobotics IBIS™ drone-based inspection system, Clobotics Crawler™ for internal inspections, and Clobotics IRIS™ for data management. In retail, the company offers Retail Insight as a Service and the SmartView Cooler Solution (Clobotics, 2024).

The company has raised a total of $83.9 million across multiple funding rounds, with key investors including CDIB Capital Group, GP Capital, and Tiger Global Management. Clobotics is recognized as a "soonicorn," indicating its potential to achieve unicorn status soon (Tracxn, 2021-08-17).

Clobotics has been acknowledged for its innovative solutions, being featured in the CNBC Upstart 100 List and Tracxn’s Top Renewable Energy Tech Firms. The company has also expanded its capabilities through acquisitions, such as Danish Blade Service and Finetune, to strengthen its position in the wind repair market (Clobotics, 2024).
