About Clobotics

Clobotics is a technology company founded in 2016, headquartered in Shanghai, China. The company specializes in providing advanced solutions for the wind energy and retail industries through the use of computer vision, artificial intelligence, and data analytics. Clobotics operates globally, focusing on automating inspection and maintenance processes to enhance operational efficiency and safety.

Products and Services

Wind Services

Clobotics offers comprehensive inspection, repair, and maintenance solutions for wind turbines. Their flagship product, the Clobotics IBIS™, is an autonomous drone system designed for rapid and detailed inspections of turbine blades. The system can inspect all three blades of a turbine in just 18-25 minutes, providing high-quality data processed and made available online within 3-5 working days. Other products include the Clobotics IRIS™ portal for managing inspection data and the Clobotics Crawler™, a robot for internal inspections of turbine blades.

Retail Services

In the retail sector, Clobotics leverages computer vision and IoT devices to provide insights and analytics for physical retail environments. Their services include Retail Insight as a Service, which collects and processes promotional execution data in real-time, and a Smartphone App for convenient access to services and insights.

Business Model and Customers

Clobotics operates primarily in the B2B SaaS space, providing services that include drone data acquisition, cloud-based data analysis, and IT consulting. The company is recognized for its innovative use of artificial intelligence and computer vision technologies to enhance operational efficiency in wind turbine inspections and retail analytics (How Clobotics Overcame Multi-Cloud and Massive File Storage Challenges, 2024).

Financials and Scale

Clobotics has raised a total of $83.9 million across six funding rounds, with the latest being a Series A round of $30.9 million in March 2021, backed by investors such as Tiger Global Management and GGV Capital (Tracxn, 2021). As of December 31, 2023, Clobotics had 11 employees (Tracxn, 2021).

Key Personnel

  • George Yan, CEO: George Yan has emphasized the company's vision to create powerful, reliable, and easy-to-use inspection systems. He has highlighted the importance of building systems that allow customers to conduct inspections independently, saving time and money (Announcing Clobotics IBIS™ 4.8, 2024).

  • Yan Ke, Chief Technology Officer: Yan Ke has underscored the significance of data collection in wind turbine operations, noting that the Clobotics Crawler sets a new standard for high-quality, automated visual documentation of the inside of blades (Clobotics Wind Services Announces Its New Clobotics Crawler™ Robot, 2024).

News

Product Launches

Clobotics Crawler™

On May 2, 2024, Clobotics announced the launch of its Clobotics Crawler™ robot, designed for inspecting the internal conditions of wind turbine blades. The robot integrates with the Clobotics IRIS™ cloud data platform, which has supported external visual inspections of over 100,000 wind turbines since 2017 (Clobotics Wind Services Announces Its New Clobotics Crawler™ Robot, 2024).

Clobotics IBIS™ 4.8

On April 11, 2024, Clobotics released the latest version of its autonomous drone-based inspection system, Clobotics IBIS™ 4.8. This version incorporates the Sony ILX-LR1 commercial camera, enhancing the speed, cost, and quality of external visual inspections of wind turbine blades (Announcing Clobotics IBIS™ 4.8, 2024).
