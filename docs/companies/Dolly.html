About Dolly

Dolly is a company founded on January 1, 2014, that specializes in providing on-demand moving and delivery services through a mobile app. The company operates in 45 cities across the United States and has completed millions of moves and deliveries. Dolly's services include apartment moves, furniture moving, storage moves, and junk removal. The company connects customers with independent contractors, known as Helpers, who use pickup trucks, cargo vans, and box trucks to perform these services (Crunchbase, 2025).

Dolly's business model is primarily B2C, serving individual consumers who need assistance with moving and delivery. However, it also partners with major retailers such as Best Buy, Williams-Sonoma, The Container Store, Crate & Barrel, and Extra Space Storage to provide delivery services, thus extending its reach into the B2B sector (About). The company does not sell physical products but offers a platform for moving and delivery services, allowing customers to schedule help through its app or website (On-Demand Moving Help & Furniture Delivery).

Dolly has raised a total of $17 million in funding, with notable rounds including $8 million in October 2015 and $7 million in May 2019 (Crunchbase, 2025). The company emphasizes customer satisfaction, boasting high average customer ratings of 4.9 out of 5 stars (Earn money with your muscle).
