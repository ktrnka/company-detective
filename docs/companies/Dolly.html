About Dolly

Dolly is a company founded on January 1, 2014, with the mission to transform the moving and delivery experience by focusing on customer control, convenience, and safety at an affordable price. The company operates in 45 cities across the United States and has completed millions of moves and deliveries. Dolly has established partnerships with major retailers such as Best Buy, Williams-Sonoma, The Container Store, Crate & Barrel, and Extra Space Storage, positioning itself as a leading on-demand delivery service (Crunchbase, 2025).

Dolly offers a range of on-demand moving and delivery services, including apartment moves, furniture delivery, labor-only services, junk haul away, and retail delivery. The company primarily operates through its mobile application, which allows users to schedule and manage their moving and delivery needs. Dolly's services are facilitated by independent contractors known as "Dolly Helpers" and "Dolly Hands," who use their own vehicles to provide these services (Dolly's website, 2025).

Dolly serves a diverse customer base, including individual consumers and retail partners, and boasts an impressive average customer rating of 4.9 out of 5 stars nationwide. The company is part of the Taskrabbit family, which enhances its resources and culture. Dolly promotes a flexible work environment for its Helpers and Hands, allowing them to choose their schedules and jobs (Dolly's website, 2025).
