About LiquidMetal AI

LiquidMetal AI is a technology company founded in 2024, located in Mountain View, California (Crunchbase, 2025). The company specializes in providing intelligent storage solutions that integrate AI capabilities into data management, aiming to simplify the process for developers to build AI-powered applications. LiquidMetal AI operates primarily in the B2B sector, offering products and services that enhance data management and analytics capabilities.

Products and Services

LiquidMetal AI offers several key products:

  • SmartBuckets: A storage SaaS service that adds a smart layer to object storage, utilizing AI for automatic data indexing and understanding. It features natural language querying, minimal coding requirements, and supports multiple programming languages. A free tier is available, offering 10GB of storage and a monthly allotment of query tokens (SmartBuckets by LiquidMetal AI).

  • Raindrop: An agentic AI platform for defining applications and managing their deployment. It includes a central data repository, automated metadata management, and tools for data lineage and collaboration (Raindrop Catalog Service).

  • Agentic AI Building Blocks: This includes advanced document processing, intelligent information retrieval, and cognitive architecture for maintaining context and making informed decisions (Agentic AI Platform).

The company also offers professional services to help clients develop custom AI applications, leveraging their expertise in AI-infused applications.

Business Model and Customers

LiquidMetal AI generates revenue through its SaaS offerings and professional services. The company serves a diverse range of customers looking to integrate AI capabilities into their data management and application development processes. The products are distributed through their website, which also serves as a platform for user engagement and community building (Privacy Policy, LiquidMetal AI).

Company Culture and Mission

LiquidMetal AI fosters a culture of innovation and collaboration, encouraging developers to explore and create robust AI solutions. The company's mission is to transform data storage and management through intelligent solutions, making it easier for developers to leverage AI in their applications.
