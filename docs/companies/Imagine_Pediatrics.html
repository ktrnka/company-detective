About Imagine Pediatrics

Imagine Pediatrics is a Nashville-based pediatrician-led medical group that specializes in providing 24/7 virtual-first care for children with complex medical conditions and special health care needs. Founded in 2022, the company aims to improve access to high-quality pediatric care by surrounding children with a multidisciplinary team that includes doctors, nurses, social workers, and therapists. The organization currently operates in Texas and Florida, serving over 20,000 children and families (Business Wire, 2023-11-29).

Services and Products

Imagine Pediatrics offers a comprehensive suite of services designed to support children with conditions such as asthma, ADHD, and technology dependence. Their services include: - Virtual Care: Accessible 24/7 through a mobile app or by calling (833) 208-7770. - Care Coordination: Assistance in navigating complex medical needs, coordinating with specialists, and managing medications. - Support for Caregivers: Resources and support for private duty nurses and caregivers.

Business Model

Imagine Pediatrics employs a value-based, population health payment model, partnering with Medicaid managed care organizations to deliver care at no additional cost to patients. This model allows the company to take full risk on the total cost of care for enrolled children, aiming to reduce unnecessary hospital visits and improve overall health outcomes (Healthcare Innovation, 2023-12-19).

Revenue and Scale

The company collaborates with health plans such as Superior Health Plan and UnitedHealthcare Community Plan to enhance care for children with medical complexities. In its first 10 months, Imagine Pediatrics conducted over 50,000 care interactions, resulting in a 15% reduction in inpatient admissions and a 20% reduction in total cost of care for engaged patients (Business Wire, 2023-11-29).

Distribution

Imagine Pediatrics' services are distributed through a mobile app and direct phone access, ensuring that families can receive immediate assistance whenever needed. The enrollment process involves an initial call to determine eligibility, followed by app download and a 15-minute introductory call to understand the child's needs.

Company Evolution

Since its founding in 2022, Imagine Pediatrics has rapidly expanded its services and partnerships, positioning itself as a leader in the virtual pediatric care market. The company plans to scale its operations and is currently in discussions with various health plan partners to expand its reach (Healthcare Innovation, 2023-12-19).

Key Personnel

George Boghos, CEO

George Boghos serves as the CEO of Imagine Pediatrics. He has a background in healthcare finance and previously co-founded AIM Clinics, which provided behavioral analysis services to children with autism. Boghos emphasizes the need for innovation in pediatric care, particularly for children with chronic conditions, stating, "Pediatrics generally is one of the last places for innovation in healthcare" (Healthcare Innovation, 2023-12-19). His leadership is focused on creating a supportive environment for families and improving health outcomes through a virtual-first model.

Funding and Growth

Imagine Pediatrics was founded with the support of Rubicon Founders, a Nashville-based venture incubator focused on healthcare innovation. The company has raised funding from Oak HC/FT, positioning it for growth and expansion into additional states in the near future (Tracxn, 2023-10-11).

Market Position and Competitors

Imagine Pediatrics ranks 27th among 105 active competitors in the virtual pediatric care space, with notable competitors including Teladoc and Plushcare (Tracxn, 2023-10-11). The company differentiates itself through its focus on children with complex medical needs and its innovative care delivery model, which aims to reduce hospital visits and improve care coordination.
