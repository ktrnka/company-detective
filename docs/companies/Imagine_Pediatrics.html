About Imagine Pediatrics

Imagine Pediatrics is a pediatrician-led medical group that provides 24/7 virtual-first care for children with special health care needs. The organization focuses on offering an extra layer of medical, behavioral, and social support to help children avoid unnecessary hospital visits and thrive at home. The company primarily serves children covered by Medicaid Managed Care Organization (MCO) plans, particularly those with complex health needs, frequent hospitalizations, and multiple medications (Imagine Pediatrics, 2023).

Company History

Imagine Pediatrics was founded to address the challenges faced by children with complex medical needs and their families. The organization emphasizes coordinated care and support, ensuring that families have access to the resources they need. The company currently operates in Texas and Florida, with plans to expand to additional states in the future (Imagine Pediatrics, 2023).

Services Offered

  • Virtual Pediatric Care: Provides 24/7 access to pediatricians, nurses, and other healthcare professionals through a mobile app.
  • Care Coordination: Assists in managing referrals, appointments, and communication with existing healthcare providers.
  • Support for Special Needs: Offers tailored services for children with complex, chronic medical, behavioral, and social needs.
  • Emergency Support: Provides immediate assistance during acute episodes, including virtual consultations and home visits when necessary (Imagine Pediatrics, 2023).

Products

The primary product is a mobile app that allows parents and caregivers to connect with healthcare providers, manage appointments, and access resources (Imagine Pediatrics, 2023).
