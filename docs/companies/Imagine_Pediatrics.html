About Imagine Pediatrics

Imagine Pediatrics is a virtual healthcare provider specializing in pediatric care for children with complex medical conditions. The company was founded in 2022 and is based in Nashville, Tennessee. It offers a virtual-first care model that provides 24/7 access to pediatricians, nurses, social workers, and other clinicians through a mobile app. The primary focus is on children with special health care needs, aiming to reduce unnecessary emergency room visits and enhance the quality of life for these children by providing continuous care and support (Tracxn, 2023-10-11).

The company primarily serves children covered by Medicaid Managed Care Organization (MCO) plans, particularly those with complex health needs. Imagine Pediatrics generates revenue by partnering with health plans and providing services to their members. Imagine Pediatrics has raised funding from Oak HC/FT and ranks 27th among 105 active competitors in the pediatric telehealth space. The competitive landscape includes companies like Teladoc and Plushcare, with over $379 million raised across 36 funding rounds involving 83 investors (Tracxn, 2023-10-11).
