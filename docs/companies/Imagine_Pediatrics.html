About Imagine Pediatrics

Imagine Pediatrics is a healthcare company based in Nashville, founded in 2022 by Taylor Beery, Zeena Johar, Meghan Haycraft, and Alison Curfman. The company specializes in providing virtual healthcare services specifically for children with complex medical conditions. Their services include 24/7 virtual and in-home pediatric care, focusing on integrated medical, behavioral, and social care. Imagine Pediatrics aims to address the fragmented healthcare system that children with special health care needs (CSHCN) often face by offering comprehensive support that includes technology-enabled care coordination and caregiver support (Tracxn, 2023-10-11).

The company operates on a B2C model, primarily serving children and their families. As of the latest reports, Imagine Pediatrics has raised a total of $37.6 million in funding, with its most recent Series C round completed on February 27, 2025. The company has achieved significant milestones, including serving over 30,000 patients across three states and reducing the Medical Cost Ratio by over 10% (Tracxn, 2023-10-11).

Imagine Pediatrics is positioned within a competitive landscape, ranked 27th among 107 active competitors, including companies like Teladoc and Plushcare. Despite the competition, the company differentiates itself through its focus on children with complex medical needs and its commitment to caregiver engagement (Tracxn, 2023-10-11).

Key Personnel

  • Taylor Beery: Co-founder of Imagine Pediatrics. Beery emphasizes the importance of engaged caregivers in the healthcare system and advocates for policies that recognize caregiving as essential work (Beery & Curfman, 2025-01-24).

  • Alison Curfman: Co-founder of Imagine Pediatrics. Curfman, along with Beery, supports innovative care models that promote caregiver engagement (Beery & Curfman, 2025-01-24).

Funding and Financials

Imagine Pediatrics has successfully raised $37.6 million in funding, with Oak HC/FT as its institutional investor. The company is currently in the Series C stage, indicating strong investor interest in its innovative approach to pediatric care (Tracxn, 2023-10-11).

Market Position and Competitors

Imagine Pediatrics operates in a competitive market with 107 active competitors. Its primary competitors include Teladoc, Plushcare, and Firefly Health. Despite the competition, Imagine Pediatrics differentiates itself through its focus on children with complex medical needs and its commitment to caregiver engagement (Tracxn, 2023-10-11).

Achievements

The company has achieved significant milestones, including:

  • Serving over 30,000 patients across three states.
  • Achieving a 10%+ reduction in Medical Cost Ratio.
  • Receiving an 87+ Net Promoter Score from patients and caregivers.
  • Preventing over 5,000 emergency and urgent care visits through timely primary interventions (Beery & Curfman, 2025-01-24).

Conclusion

Imagine Pediatrics is positioned as a leader in the virtual healthcare space for children with special health care needs. With a strong funding base, innovative care model, and a commitment to caregiver engagement, the company is well-equipped to address the challenges faced by CSHCN and their families. As it continues to grow, prospective candidates and investors may find significant opportunities in its mission to transform pediatric care.
