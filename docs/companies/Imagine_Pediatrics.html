About Imagine Pediatrics

Imagine Pediatrics is a pediatrician-led medical group founded in 2022, based in Nashville, Tennessee. The company specializes in providing 24/7 virtual-first care for children with special health care needs, particularly those covered by Medicaid Managed Care Organization (MCO) plans. Their services are designed to support families by offering medical, behavioral, and social care, aiming to reduce unnecessary hospital visits and improve the quality of life for children with complex medical conditions (Business Wire, 2023-11-29).

The company employs a multidisciplinary team, including pediatricians, nurses, social workers, and therapists, who work collaboratively with existing healthcare providers. Imagine Pediatrics serves over 20,000 children and families in Texas and Florida, with plans to expand to other states (Frequently Asked Questions, 2023). The services are integrated into the patients' existing health plan benefits, ensuring no additional costs for eligible families (Business Wire, 2023-11-29).

Imagine Pediatrics generates revenue through partnerships with Medicaid managed care organizations, implementing a value-based payment model that aligns incentives to improve health outcomes. This model allows the company to take full risk on the total cost of care for enrolled children (HCI Innovation Group, 2023-12-19).

Key Personnel

  • George Boghos: CEO of Imagine Pediatrics, with a background in healthcare finance and experience in scaling healthcare services. He previously co-founded AIM Clinics, which provided behavioral analysis services to children with autism (HCI Innovation Group, 2023-12-19).

  • Diana Yang: Chief Financial Officer, with a background in pediatric healthcare startups (Imagine Pediatrics - Leadership, 2023).

  • Kari O'Rourke: Chief Operating Officer, with extensive experience in clinical operations (Imagine Pediatrics - Leadership, 2023).

  • Taylor Beery: Executive focused on improving care delivery for children with medical complexity (Imagine Pediatrics - Leadership, 2023).

Expansion and Impact

Imagine Pediatrics has conducted over 50,000 care interactions in its first ten months, resulting in a 15% reduction in inpatient admissions and a 20% decrease in total care costs for engaged patients. The company plans to expand its services into additional states in 2024, with ongoing discussions with various health plan partners (Business Wire, 2023-11-29).

Partnerships and Collaborations

The company partners with Medicaid managed care organizations, including Superior Health Plan and UnitedHealthcare Community Plan, to implement a value-based payment model. This approach aligns incentives to improve health outcomes and allows Imagine Pediatrics to take full risk on the total cost of care for enrolled children (HCI Innovation Group, 2023-12-19).

Funding and Financials

Imagine Pediatrics was founded with support from Rubicon Founders, a Nashville-based venture incubator focused on healthcare innovation. The company has raised funding from institutional investors, including Oak HC/FT, although specific funding amounts have not been disclosed (Tracxn, 2023-10-11).
