About Imagine Pediatrics

Imagine Pediatrics is a pediatrician-led medical group that specializes in providing virtual-first care for children with special healthcare needs. The company operates primarily in Texas and Florida, with plans to expand to other states. Their services are designed to support families by offering 24/7 access to a multidisciplinary team of healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, social workers, and other clinicians. The goal is to help children avoid unnecessary emergency room visits and spend more time at home and in their communities (Imagine Pediatrics, 2023).

Services Offered

  • 24/7 Virtual Pediatric Care: Provides immediate access to pediatric nurses and doctors via a mobile app or phone call.
  • Care Coordination: Assists in managing referrals, medication refills, and specialist appointments.
  • Support for Caregivers: Offers help for parents and private duty nurses in navigating healthcare needs and accessing resources.
  • Behavioral and Social Support: Addresses the holistic needs of children and their families (Imagine Pediatrics, 2023).

Customer Base

Imagine Pediatrics primarily serves children covered by Medicaid Managed Care Organization (MCO) plans, focusing on those with complex, chronic medical, behavioral, and social needs (Imagine Pediatrics, 2023).
