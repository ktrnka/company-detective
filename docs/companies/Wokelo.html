About Wokelo

Wokelo is an AI-powered investment research platform founded on February 9, 2023, by Siddhant Masson and Saswat Nanda. The company is headquartered in Seattle, Washington, with an additional presence in Bengaluru, India. Wokelo specializes in automating complex research workflows, such as due diligence, sector research, and portfolio monitoring, using its proprietary large language model (LLM)-based technology. This platform is designed to enhance the efficiency and speed of data analysis and intelligence gathering, which is crucial for financial decision-making in sectors like private equity, investment banking, and consulting (KPMG, 2023).

Wokelo has raised a total of $5.5 million in funding since its inception, with notable investors including KPMG Ventures, Array Ventures, and Untapped Capital. The company currently employs 13 people and claims to have served over 35 clients, including prominent firms in private equity and venture capital (VCCircle, 2023).

Wokelo's platform is utilized by leading private equity funds, investment banks, consulting firms, and corporate strategy teams. The company aims to support data-driven decision-making, thereby delivering research solutions that are not only efficient but also accurate and thorough (TechJockey, 2025).

Key Personnel

  • Siddhant Masson: Co-founder and CEO. He has been instrumental in driving Wokelo's vision and growth, focusing on innovation and the application of AI technologies to streamline investment research processes (KPMG, 2023).

News

Funding and Investment

  • On October 1, 2023, Wokelo announced a minority equity investment from KPMG LLP, which aims to enhance the platform's capabilities by combining Wokelo's AI-driven insights with KPMG's deal advisory and strategy expertise. This collaboration is expected to empower companies to make faster and more informed investment decisions (KPMG, 2023).
  • Wokelo raised $4 million in its seed funding round, with participation from AI-focused venture capital firms such as Geek Ventures and Rebellion Ventures, alongside existing investors (VCCircle, 2023).

Product Features and Capabilities

  • Wokelo's platform utilizes advanced LLM technology to automate the collection, curation, synthesis, and triangulation of data, producing client-ready outputs in a fraction of the time traditionally required. The platform is particularly beneficial for generating detailed due diligence reports for mergers and acquisitions, allowing users to obtain comprehensive documents in minutes rather than weeks (KPMG, 2023).

Partnerships and Collaborations

  • The partnership with KPMG is a significant development for Wokelo, as it aligns the company with a global leader in audit, tax, and advisory services. KPMG's Head of Deal Advisory, Carole Streicher, emphasized the importance of AI in enhancing deal-making processes, stating, "Together, Wokelo and KPMG are setting a new standard in data analysis and intelligence gathering" (KPMG, 2023).
