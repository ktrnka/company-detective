About Wokelo

Wokelo is a technology company founded on February 9, 2023, that specializes in leveraging artificial intelligence to enhance business processes, particularly in the realm of investment research. The company is based in the United States with an additional office in Bengaluru, India. Wokelo's primary product is a Gen-AI powered investment research platform designed to automate complex research workflows such as due diligence, sector research, and portfolio monitoring. The platform uses proprietary large language model (LLM)-based technology to automate and streamline data curation, synthesis, and triangulation, delivering client-ready outputs (Crunchbase, 2024).

Wokelo operates in a B2B model, serving a diverse clientele that includes private equity funds, investment banks, consulting firms, and corporates. The company has served over 35 clients and currently employs 13 people (KPMG, 2023) (VCCircle, 2023). Wokelo generates revenue through its AI-driven platform, which is sold to businesses looking to enhance their operational efficiency and decision-making processes.

The company has raised a total of $5 million in funding, with a $4 million seed round completed on October 7, 2024, and a $1 million round on June 20, 2023. This funding supports the development of its technology and market capabilities (Crunchbase, 2024).

Wokelo is described by third parties as a promising player in the AI-driven research and due diligence market, with significant backing from reputable investors like KPMG Ventures and Array Ventures. The company's collaboration with KPMG is particularly noteworthy, as it aims to set a new standard in data analysis and intelligence gathering (KPMG, 2023).

Key Personnel

Wokelo was founded by Siddhant Masson and Saswat Nanda. Siddhant Masson serves as the CEO and has emphasized the company's growth trajectory and its ability to transform large and diverse data sets into actionable insights (KPMG, 2023). Information about Saswat Nanda's role and background is not explicitly provided in the available sources.

News

Funding and Investment

Wokelo raised $4 million in a seed funding round in October 2024, with participation from KPMG Ventures, Array Ventures, and other AI-focused venture capital firms. This investment includes a minority equity stake from KPMG, which will allow Wokelo to combine its AI-driven insights with KPMG's deal advisory and strategy expertise (KPMG, 2023) (VCCircle, 2023).

Partnerships

The partnership with KPMG is a significant development for Wokelo, as it aims to enhance the company's capabilities in data analysis and intelligence gathering. Carole Streicher, Head of Deal Advisory at KPMG US, highlighted the potential for this collaboration to provide more comprehensive, actionable, and strategic advice (KPMG, 2023).

Product Developments

Wokelo's platform is designed to streamline the due diligence process by processing data from various sources, enabling more time for in-depth analysis. The AI technology can generate detailed due diligence reports in a fraction of the time required by traditional methods. For example, Yohei Nakajima, a general partner at Untapped Capital, noted that Wokelo can produce a 20-50 page report in just 30 minutes (Poppy, 2024).

Market Position

Wokelo operates in a competitive landscape that includes other AI-driven platforms like Dili, Ansarada, and AlphaSense. However, its proprietary LLM technology is a unique selling proposition that enhances the accuracy and speed of data analysis (Dili, 2024).
