About Wokelo

Wokelo is a technology company founded on February 9, 2023, by Siddhant Masson and Saswat Nanda. It operates as a Gen-AI powered investment research platform, focusing on automating complex research workflows such as due diligence, sector research, and portfolio monitoring. The company is based in the United States, with an additional office in Bengaluru, India (Crunchbase, 2025) (KPMG, 2023).

Wokelo's primary product is an AI-driven platform that leverages proprietary large language model (LLM) technology to automate the collection, curation, synthesis, and triangulation of data, delivering client-ready outputs. This platform is particularly beneficial for private equity funds, investment banks, consulting firms, and corporates, enabling them to make data-driven decisions more efficiently (Crunchbase, 2025).

The company has raised a total of $5.5 million in funding, with a significant $4 million seed investment in October 2024. Notable investors include KPMG Ventures, Array Ventures, and Untapped Capital (VCCircle, 2023) (Crunchbase, 2025). Wokelo has served over 35 clients and employs approximately 13 people (KPMG, 2023).

News

Investment and Partnerships

  • On October 1, 2023, Wokelo announced a minority equity investment from KPMG LLP. This partnership aims to enhance Wokelo's AI technology by integrating it with KPMG's deal advisory and strategy expertise, setting a new standard in data analysis and intelligence gathering (KPMG, 2023).

Product and Market Position

  • Wokelo's platform is gaining traction among large enterprises for its ability to transform large and diverse data sets into actionable insights. The platform's efficiency in generating comprehensive due diligence reports in about 30 minutes is a significant advantage over traditional methods (Chubb, 2024).

  • The KPMG 2024 M&A Mid-year survey highlighted that 42% of dealmakers are using GenAI in their processes, indicating a growing market for Wokelo's services (KPMG, 2023).

Funding and Growth

  • Wokelo raised $4 million in a funding round on October 7, 2024, to further develop its technology and expand its market presence (Crunchbase, 2025).

  • The company is actively hiring across various roles, as indicated by a LinkedIn post from Siddhant Masson on July 18, 2024, reflecting its growth and expansion efforts (LinkedIn, 2024).

Industry Impact

  • An article from December 11, 2023, discusses how Wokelo's platform streamlines due diligence processes, cuts costs, and automates 40% of tasks, highlighting its impact on reducing grunt work in the industry (Renato Capelj, 2023).

Wokelo is positioning itself as a leader in AI-driven research and intelligence gathering, with significant funding, strategic partnerships, and a robust product offering. The company's innovative approach and growth potential make it a noteworthy player in the AI and investment research sectors.
