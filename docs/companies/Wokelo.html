About Wokelo

Wokelo is an AI-powered research platform founded on February 9, 2023, by Siddhant Masson and Saswat Nanda. The company is headquartered in Seattle, with an additional office in Bengaluru, India. Wokelo specializes in automating complex research workflows such as due diligence, sector research, and portfolio monitoring. The platform leverages proprietary large language model (LLM) technology to automate the collection, curation, synthesis, and triangulation of data, delivering client-ready outputs. Wokelo primarily serves private equity funds, investment banks, consulting firms, and corporates, helping them make data-driven decisions (Crunchbase, 2025) (KPMG, 2023).

Wokelo has served over 35 clients and currently employs 13 people. The company has raised a total of $5.5 million in funding, with $4 million secured in October 2024 and $1 million in June 2023. This funding supports the development of its technology and enhances its capabilities in data processing and analysis (VCCircle, 2023) (Crunchbase, 2025).

Wokelo's products are distributed as a platform that streamlines the due diligence process by processing data from various sources, enabling clients to focus on in-depth analysis. The platform is designed to generate detailed reports quickly, significantly reducing the time required for traditional analysis (Chubb, 2024).

Funding and Investment

Wokelo recently raised $4 million in seed funding, with participation from KPMG Ventures, Array Ventures, and other investors. This investment is intended to support the further development of Wokelo's technology and enhance its data processing and analysis capabilities (VCCircle, 2023). The investment from KPMG Ventures is particularly significant, as it aligns Wokelo's AI-driven insights with KPMG's deal advisory and strategy expertise, creating a partnership that aims to improve strategic decision-making for clients (KPMG, 2023).

Strategic Partnerships

The collaboration with KPMG is a key strategic move for Wokelo, combining its LLM-based technology with KPMG's extensive experience in deal advisory. This partnership is expected to enhance the value delivered to clients, allowing them to evaluate deals with greater confidence and improve their strategic and financial positioning (KPMG, 2023).

Product Developments

Wokelo's platform is designed to streamline the due diligence process by processing data from various sources, enabling clients to focus on in-depth analysis. The platform utilizes advanced AI techniques to generate detailed reports in a fraction of the time it would take traditional analysts. For instance, an investor noted that by simply entering a company name, they could receive a comprehensive report within 30 minutes, which would typically take analysts two weeks to compile (Chubb, 2024).

Wokelo's proprietary "cognitive engine" is a standout feature that allows the platform to sift through vast amounts of data to produce concise and customized reports without hallucinations. This capability is crucial for clients in sectors such as private equity, venture capital, investment banking, and management consulting, where timely and accurate data analysis is essential (Inc42, 2025).

Market Position and Scale

Wokelo is positioned within the rapidly growing AI and data analytics market, which has seen significant investment and interest in recent years. The company has raised a total of $5.5 million since its inception, indicating strong investor confidence in its business model and technology (Inc42, 2025). The demand for AI-driven solutions in data analysis is expected to continue growing, providing Wokelo with ample opportunities for expansion.

Executive Insights

Siddhant Masson, CEO of Wokelo, emphasized the company's commitment to transforming data into actionable insights, stating, "Wokelo continues to gain traction among large enterprises by facilitating the seamless transformation of large and diverse data sets into actionable insights" (KPMG, 2023).
