About Pave

Pave is a company focused on enhancing financial inclusion for underserved consumers and businesses by providing innovative credit assessment tools. Founded on January 1, 2020, Pave aims to create a future where equitable credit is accessible to everyone, addressing the needs of over 100 million U.S. consumers and businesses that are financially underserved due to unrecognized data by traditional financial systems (Crunchbase, 2025).

Pave primarily operates in the B2B sector, serving lenders and financial institutions. The company offers a suite of tools and analytics designed to help these clients assess credit risk and improve approval rates for underserved applicants. Their key offerings include:

  • Cashflow Analytics: Provides real-time insights into income, expenses, and liabilities to enhance lending decisions.
  • Cashflow Attributes: Generates insights into a borrower's stability, affordability, assets, and willingness to repay.
  • Cashflow Scores: Predicts the likelihood of borrowers making their first payments on various credit products.
  • Integration with Data Aggregators: Allows lenders to leverage existing data sources for enhanced analytics (Pave, 2025).

Pave's solutions are applicable in various lending scenarios, such as cash advances, credit cards, personal loans, and small dollar loans. The company is committed to data security, employing a multi-layered approach that includes encryption, access controls, and regular security audits. Pave is SOC-2 Type II certified, ensuring compliance with industry standards for data protection (Pave, 2025).

Pave's mission is to empower developers to create personalized financial experiences with minimal friction and cost, ultimately redefining how creditworthiness is assessed in the financial ecosystem (Pave, 2025).
