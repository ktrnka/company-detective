About Pave

Pave is a technology company founded on January 1, 2020, with a mission to enhance financial inclusion by providing cashflow analytics tools for lenders. The company aims to ensure equitable credit access for individuals and businesses, particularly those underserved by traditional financial systems. Pave's platform allows lenders to assess affordability, reduce defaults, and increase approvals through customizable solutions tailored to specific lending criteria (Crunchbase, 2025).

Pave offers several products designed to empower lenders:

  • Cashflow Analytics: Provides real-time insights into income, expenses, and liabilities to help lenders make informed decisions.
  • Cashflow Attributes: Generates attributes that give insights into a borrower's stability, affordability, assets, and willingness to repay.
  • Cashflow Scores: Predicts the likelihood of borrowers making their first few payments on various credit products, helping lenders assess risk more accurately.
  • Integration with Data Aggregators: Compatible with various data aggregators, allowing lenders to leverage existing data sources without ingesting personally identifiable information (PII).

Pave primarily serves lenders, including consumer credit providers and fleet card providers, by equipping them with advanced analytics and tools to enhance risk management and promote growth. Their solutions are applicable across various lending types, including cash advances, personal loans, credit cards, and small dollar loans (Pave, Pavefi).

The company operates as a fully remote team, emphasizing diversity and self-starters passionate about financial inclusion. Pave is SOC-2 Type II certified, employing a multi-layered approach to security, including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits (Pave, Pavefi).

Pave has raised a total of $6 million in funding as of July 1, 2023 (Crunchbase, 2025).
