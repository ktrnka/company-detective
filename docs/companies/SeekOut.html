About SeekOut

SeekOut is a talent intelligence platform founded on August 1, 2016, and is headquartered in Redmond, Washington. The company was co-founded by Anoop Gupta, Vikas Manocha, John Tippett, and Aravind Bala. As of December 2023, SeekOut employs 191 people, a reduction from previous years, which may indicate restructuring or market adjustments (Tracxn, 2021-08-17). The company operates in the B2B SaaS sector, focusing on high-tech markets, and has raised a total of $186 million in funding across several rounds, with its latest Series C round in January 2022 valuing the company at $1.2 billion (Tracxn, 2021-08-17).

SeekOut's products and services are designed to enhance talent acquisition and management processes. The company offers several key products:

  • SeekOut Recruit: A software solution for talent acquisition that helps teams find candidates through various channels.
  • SeekOut Grow: A talent management tool that includes features like an internal talent marketplace and career pathing.
  • SeekOut Spot: An AI-driven recruiting service that combines AI agents with human recruiters to streamline hiring.
  • SeekOut Assist: A tool that uses AI to generate targeted searches and personalized messages for candidates.

SeekOut serves over 700 customers across various sectors, including technology, defense, and pharmaceuticals. The platform is particularly noted for its effectiveness in sourcing diverse candidates (Tracxn, 2021-08-17). The company distributes its products through partnerships with various talent acquisition platforms, such as iCIMS, Greenhouse, and Workday, among others (SeekOut, 2024-07-16).

Key Personnel

  • Anoop Gupta: Co-founder and CEO of SeekOut. Gupta has a background in computer science and entrepreneurship, having previously worked at Microsoft and Stanford University. He emphasizes the importance of adapting to market changes and leveraging AI to enhance recruitment processes (Garnick, Madrona, 2025-04-24).

News

Layoffs and Restructuring

In May 2024, SeekOut laid off 30% of its workforce, a move that was part of a broader trend of layoffs in the tech industry. This reduction in workforce may reflect internal challenges or market adjustments (TechCrunch, 2024-05-26). The layoffs were reported by multiple sources, including GeekWire and The Business Journals (GeekWire, 2024-05-26) (The Business Journals, 2024-05-26).

Product Innovations

SeekOut has introduced several innovations, including SeekOut Spot, which uses agentic AI to significantly reduce the time from job description to qualified candidates, reportedly from 45 days to just three. This innovation allows recruiters to focus on strategic tasks while the AI handles labor-intensive aspects of candidate sourcing (Garnick, Madrona, 2025-04-24).

Funding and Valuation

SeekOut has raised a total of $186 million in funding, with its most recent Series C round in January 2022 raising $115 million and valuing the company at $1.2 billion (Tracxn, 2021-08-17).

Market Position

SeekOut is a key player in the talent intelligence market, competing with 33 active competitors, including Bryq and Cangrade. The company's innovative use of AI helps maintain its strong market position (Tracxn, 2021-08-17).

Employee and Customer Sentiment

SeekOut is recognized for its commitment to diversity and employee satisfaction, promoting a culture of kindness, gratitude, and curiosity. However, the recent reduction in employee count may raise questions about internal challenges or market adjustments (SeekOut, 2024-12-18).

Conclusion

SeekOut is positioned as a leader in the AI-driven recruitment space, with a strong focus on talent intelligence and innovative solutions like SeekOut Spot. The company's significant funding and valuation reflect its potential for growth and impact in the recruitment industry. As it continues to evolve, SeekOut's commitment to leveraging AI for better hiring practices and employee management will be crucial for its future success.
