About Veritone

Veritone, Inc. is a prominent provider of AI-driven solutions, founded in 2014. The company is headquartered in Costa Mesa, California, and operates in various sectors, including media, entertainment, public safety, and talent acquisition. Veritone's flagship platform, aiWARE™, orchestrates a diverse ecosystem of machine learning models to transform data into actionable intelligence, enhancing decision-making and operational efficiency for its clients (Veritone, Business Wire, 2024).

Veritone primarily operates as a B2B company, serving a diverse clientele that includes major brands in media and entertainment, public safety agencies, and government entities. The company has established itself as a leader in human-centered enterprise AI solutions, with a strong emphasis on ethical practices and human-centered technology (Veritone, Business Wire, 2024).

The company generates revenue through a combination of software licensing, subscription-based models, and content licensing services. In Q1 2024, Veritone reported total revenue of $31.6 million, a 4.5% increase from the previous year, with an annual recurring revenue (ARR) of $72.1 million (Veritone, Business Wire, 2024).

Veritone's products and services are distributed through direct sales and partnerships. The company has formed strategic collaborations with organizations such as Amazon Web Services (AWS) and Eightfold AI to enhance its offerings and expand its market reach (Veritone, Business Wire, 2024).

Key Personnel

Ryan Steelberg serves as the CEO and Chairman of Veritone. He has a rich background in technology and entrepreneurship, having co-founded several successful ventures, including AdForce and dMarc, which was acquired by Google. Steelberg emphasizes the importance of ethical AI and the company's commitment to responsible technology use (Makridis, Zenger News, 2024).

Partnerships and Collaborations

  1. Midwest Public Safety Partnership: Veritone has partnered with Midwest Public Safety to expand its iDEMS solution, enhancing AI applications for law enforcement across the Midwest (Veritone, Business Wire, 2024).

  2. Collaboration with Eightfold AI: The integration of Veritone's global job distribution software with Eightfold AI aims to improve recruitment efficiency for talent acquisition teams (Veritone, Business Wire, 2024).

  3. NCAA Agreement: Veritone has been named the official global archive of record and exclusive video licensing agent for the NCAA, leveraging its Digital Media Hub to manage and monetize NCAA championship content (Veritone, SVG Staff, 2024).

  4. Strategic Collaboration with AWS: A three-year agreement with Amazon Web Services (AWS) aims to accelerate cloud-native AI and generative AI innovations for various sectors (Veritone, Business Wire, 2024).

Product Innovations

  1. aiWARE™: This platform is central to Veritone's offerings, enabling real-time processing and analysis of audio, video, and text data. It supports various applications, including digital asset management and evidence management for public safety (Veritone, Business Wire, 2024).

  2. Ask Veri: A new generative AI chat tool that allows users to extract insights from media archives, enhancing the usability of Veritone's Digital Media Hub (Blakeley, Research Live, 2024).

  3. VocaliD Technology: Recognized with the Netty Award for "Best Use of AI for Accessibility," this technology enhances communication for individuals with speech disabilities (Veritone, Business Wire, 2024).

Financial Performance

In Q1 2024, Veritone reported total revenue of $31.6 million, a 4.5% increase from the previous year. The company has also implemented restructuring initiatives aimed at optimizing its organizational structure, which included a workforce reduction of approximately 14% to drive profitability (Veritone, Business Wire, 2024).
